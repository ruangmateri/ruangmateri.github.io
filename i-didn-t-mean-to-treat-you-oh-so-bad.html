15. i would treat you if i had much money . these sentences mean


Terjemahan:

Aku akan mentraktirmu jika aku memiliki banyak uang.

Kalimat ini menggunakan conditional sentence tipe 2, yang artinya hal tersebut mustahil atau sulit terjadi (reality of conditional sentences).

What is the meaning of this conditional sentence?

i would treat you if i had much money.

_______________

Jawaban :

The fact is I don't have much money , so i can't treat you.

Penjelasan :

Kalimat diatas merupakan Conditional type ke 2, dimana adanya kebohongan ( Pengandaian yang tidak terwujud) di masa sekarang atau masa mendatang ( Untrue in the present and future ). Conditional type 2  merupakan kondisi yang berlawanan / kontras dengan faktanya.

Jika 2 klausa yang terdapat dalam kalimat Conditional nya bentuk positif, Kalimat Faktanya akan berlawanan yaitu bentuk negatif simple present .

Contoh :

If i had some money, i would buy the new car

=> The fact : I don't have some money, so i can't buy the new car

26. you didn`t eat my toast (?) (+)​


Jawaban

(-) You didn't eat my toast.

(+) You ate my toast.

(?) Did you eat my toast?

Penjelasan

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian masa lampau.

Formula:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Berikut contohnya:

(+) She played chess last night.

(-) She did not play chess last night.

(?) Did she play chess last night?

(+) They went to a party.

(-) They did not go to a party.

(?) Did they go to a party?

(+) He broke up with his girlfriend.

(-) He did not brake up with his girlfriend.

(?) Did he brake up with his girlfriend?
