PASSIVE VOICE (KALIMAT PASIF)

Passive sentence adalah kalimat yang subjeknya dikenai tindakan/pekerjaan. Biasanya kata kerja dari kalimat ini diartikan menjadi "di" atau "ter"

Rumus kalimat : Subjek + aux.verb + Verb III/Past participle

Contoh kalimat passive voice :

1. Coffe is made by Febry

2. Milk tea was being made by Aqilla

3. Juice apple can be made John

a. Ukuran Lahan sebenarnya
Panjang lahan :

[tex]\frac{1}{500} = \frac{22,5\ cm}{P} \\ \\ P = 22,5\ cm \times 500 \\ \\ P= 11.250\ cm\ atau\ 112,5\ m[/tex]

Lebar Lahan :

[tex]\frac{1}{500} = \frac{12\ cm}{P} \\ \\ P = 12\ cm \times 500 \\ \\ P= 6.000\ cm\ atau\ 60\ m[/tex]

b. Perbandingan Luas Lahan pada Denah dengan Sebenarnya

Luas Denah = 22,5 cm × 12 cm = 270 cm²

Luas Sebenarnya = 11.250 cm × 6.000 cm = 67.500.000 cm²

= Luas Denah : Luas Sebenarnya
= 270 : 67.500.000 .............sama-sama dibagi dengan 270
= (270/270) : (67.500.000/270)
= 1 : 250.000

37. buatlah dialog yang menggunakan will have done dan will be doing


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: Future perfect and continous tense
Pembahasan:

Future perfect is used for stating the finished plan or action in the future.
Example:
- We will have done the movie shot in July 2019.

Future continous is used for stating the progressive plan or action in the future.
Example:
- We will still be doing the movie shot in Iceland in June 2019.

Dialogue:
Sven: Rui, are you still working on your new movie?
Rui: Well, sort of, I'm still in the middle of the shots.
Sven: what's the title again?
Rui: It's called 'The Frozen Shadow'. It's about the man who found something belongs to his past life after so many years.
Sven: Hmm, that's intriguing. When will it be released?
Rui: It should be December next year. So, we will have started promoting it afterwards.
Sven: Ah, sounds cool. How much longer will you be doing the shot?
Rui: We will be doing the shots until early October next year.
Sven: Good luck for your movie, then.
Rui: Thank you.
