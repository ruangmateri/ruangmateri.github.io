Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Present continuous
I + am + verb-ing
We/you/they + are + verb-ing
He/she/it + is + verb-ing

Contoh, someone is calling (ada org sedang nelpon)
Phone is ringing (hp sedang berdering)

"Calling" untuk simple continuous tense. Artinya kejadian yang sedang terjadi

40. apakah calling visa aman?​
