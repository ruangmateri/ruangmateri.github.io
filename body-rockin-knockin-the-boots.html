Playing in the rain has been her favorite activite since kindergarden 5. The boots have been ready to protect her body from the rain 6. Sometimes, sheina doesn't wear her boots


The proper paragraph is:

Sheina has always loved the rain (1). Playing in the rain has been her favorite activite since kindergarden (4). She has special boots and a favorite umbrella (2). The umbrella has been ready to protect her body from the rain (5). The boots have been ready to protect her body from the rain (6). Sometimes, Sheina doesn't wear her boots (10). She doesn't even user her favorite umbrella (7). She feels the rain on her skin (8). Her mother has let her to play in the rain (9). She has played in the rain many times before (3).

Pembahasan

Untuk menyusun kalimat-kalimat di atas menjadi sebuah paragraph (paragraf) maka kita harus memahami dulu arti setiap kalimat yang diberikan. Kemudian urutkan kalimat-kalimat tersebut sesuai dengan urutan logika yang bisa kita pahami sesuai dengan konteks kalimatnya seperti dalam jawaban di atas.

Once upon a time, in a far-off land, there lived a clever and cunning cat named Puss in Boots. He was famous for his quick wit and his skill in catching mice, but his true talent was his ability to charm and persuade anyone he met.
