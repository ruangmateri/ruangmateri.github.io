Recount Text

How did the writer feel when she knew that Afgan was coming to town?

JAWABAN:

She felt that she was suddenly hit by lightning or very surprised.

PEMBAHASAN:

=> Bagaimana perasaan penulis ketika dia tahu bahwa Afgan akan datang ke kota?

=> Dia merasa tiba-tiba tersambar petir atau sangat terkejut.

Jawaban di atas diperoleh dari memahami bacaan tentang "Meeting My Idol" berikut:

Afgan has always been my favourite  singer. I had always been thinking of how I would feel when I met him. Then I was  suddenly hit by lightning when I found out  Afgan was coming to town for a concert in a  local auditorium. A day before the concert,  there would be a meet-and-greet event at a  local radio station. Feeling excited, I packed  all my Afgan’s CDs to get his signature at the  event.

On that bright and sunny Saturday morning, the radio station  was full of Afganism (that’s how Afgan’s fans are called). They sat  on the chairs prepared inside the radio station’s lobby. Some stood  in rows in the front yard of the radio station. A spot inside a lobby  was prepared with a mini stage for Afgan’s singing performance  and a table for Afgan to sign Afganism’s memorabilia. Finally, after  about 40 or 50 minutes wait, Afgan showed up from inside the  radio station. He smiled and waved to all Afganism who had been  borning. How are you all?”. The  crowd went crazy. The shouts sounded like a mix of “Fine, thank  you” and screams of Afgan’s name.  

Then, he started the event by singing his hit single “Dia  dia dia”. Afganisme went even crazier; they sang along with him  throughout the song. Of course, I did too. I couldn’t take my eyes  off this amazing singer who had released three albums. When he  was finished with the song, the host announced that it was time  for autographing the memorabilia. I prepared my CDs and began  to stand in the line. When I arrived at the table, I was speechless.  It was unreal just seeing him that close. I thought it was really cool  seeing him like that because he really just felt like a normal person, which was awesome. He asked my name so that he could write it  on the CD to say “To Mia, Love Afgan”. He was also very friendly, so  I didn’t feel too nervous when I had a chance to take pictures with  him. He was just an amazing person. And it was the best day ever!

Text tentang Meeting My Idol termasuk Recount text. Recount Text adalah salah satu jenis text dalam bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan kembali tentang kejadian-kejadian atau pengalaman-pengalaman di masa lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.  

Text organization:

Orientation : berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita Event :merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis Reorientation : kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian.

Unsur Kebahasaan:

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: Afgen, I, he, we, theyMenggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: when, before, after, dan finally Menggunakan past tense, contoh: I packed all my Afgan’s CDs to get his signature at the event. Menggunakan adverbs dan adverbs of phrases, contoh: suddenly, in my house, two days ago, slowly Menggunakan adjective, contoh: amazing, cool ,awesome,smart

Pembahasan

Verb adalah kata yang mendefinisikan aksi, tindakan dan pengalaman. Yang termasuk verb dalam lirik lagu tersebut adalah Left, pour, feel, lie, get, exchange, broke, said, take back, coming, save, fall, built, pull it down, fled, scared, known, become, reach, could, go, wonder, leave, hurt, love.Adjective adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menerangkan kata benda. Yang termasuk noun dalam lrik lagu tersebut adalah Bird, tree, light, heart, head, Atlantis, town, ground, love.

Recount Text

Recount Text adalah salah satu jenis text dalam bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan kembali tentang kejadian-kejadian atau pengalaman-pengalaman di masa lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.  

Ada beberapa jenis recount text, contohnya adalah diaries, letters/postcards, journals, autobiographies dan historical recount. nah text di atas adalah salah satu contoh personal recount.

Text organization:

Orientation : berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita Sequence of events: merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis Reorientation : kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian.

Unsur Kebahasaan:

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: Afgen, I, he, we, they Menggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: when, before, after, dan finally Menggunakan past tense, contoh: I packed  all my Afgan’s CDs to get his signature at the  event. Menggunakan adverbs dan adverbs of phrases, contoh: suddenly, in my house, two days ago, slowly Menggunakan adjective, contoh: amazing, cool ,awesome,smart
