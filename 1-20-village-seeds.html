25. Dinesh and Kelvin collected some sunflower seeds. The ratio of Dinesh'sseeds to Kelvin's seeds was 9:5. Dinesh gave 45 seeds to Kelvin. The ratioof Dinesh's seeds to Kelvin's seeds became 3:5.How many sunflower seeds did Dinesh have at first?b How many sunflower seeds did Kevin have in the end?​


Jawab:

a. Dinesh had 108 seeds at first.

b. Kelvin had 105 in the end.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's use D to represent the number of Dinesh's seed originally. And K to represent Kelvin's.

D:K = 9:5

D = 1.8K

Then Dinesh gave 45 seed to Kelvin and the ratio became 3:5.

D - 45 : K + 45 = 3:5

(D-45) = 0.6 (K + 45)

D - 45 = 0.6K + 27

D = 0.6K + 72

1.8 K = 0.6K + 72

1.2K = 72

K = 60

D = 1.8K = 1.8 x 60 = 108

a. Dinesh had 108 seeds at first.

b. Kelvin had 105 in the end.
