25. What is ingredients and steps to make fried bananas ?


Ingredients

•Bananas

•8 tablespoons of rice flour

•1 teaspoon of turmeric powder

•Water

•Salt

•Oil for frying

Steps

1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, combine the rice flour, turmeric powder, and a pinch of salt. Add water slowly to make a smooth and paste - like batter. Set aside. Do not add too much water, as the batter will get thin.

2. Cut the banana half, and cut across into chunks.

3. Heat the oil in a pan over a higher heat.

4. Dip the bananas into the batter, and try to coat it evenly.

5. Fry the bananas for a few minutes or until it turn into golden brown.

6. Keep the leftover batter in the fridge, and use it for another time if you want.

Ingredients

6 medium-sized bananas (preferably pisang Abu, firm but ripe), peeled
3 tbsp rice flour
3 tbsp wheat flour
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
2 tbsp milk creamer
grated cheese
Oil for deep-frying
A pinch of salt
A pinch of sugar

Method

First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water.

Then soak bananas in the batter.

Heat the oil in a large wok.

Then drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.

When bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside.

Pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas.

Lastly, coat it with grated cheese.

Goal : How to Make Banana Cheese

2 tbsp milk creamer
grated cheese
Oil for deep-frying
