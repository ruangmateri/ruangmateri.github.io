5. The-life-people-left-a-their-town-for-new


Jawaban:

The people left their town for a new life

Pembahasan Soal:

The-life-people-left-a-their-town-for-new

Structure kalimat:The people (orang2) => Subject Left (meninggalkan) => Verb 2Their town (desa mereka) => Object For a new life (utk kehidupan yg baru => Complement

Catatan!Karena ada Verb 2 leftRumus yg digunakan adalah Past tense:

(+) S + Verb 2 + O + Complement

= The people left their town for a new life

(Orang2 meninggalkan desa mereka untuk kehidupan yg baru)

17. for-the-been-a-has - house - occupied - owner-the-by - year The correct arrangement of the jumbled words above is ... a. The house occupied for a year by the owner has been. b. The owner has been occupied by the house for a year. c.The house has been occupied by the owner for a year. d. the owner has occupied by the house been for a year​


Jawaban:

C. The house has been occupied by the owner for a year.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat tersebut menggunakan tenses Present Perfect Tenses yang berbentuk Passive Voice

Sehingga menggunakan rumus:

S (direct object) + have/has + been + past participle +/- by … (agent)

direct object = the house

agent = the owner
