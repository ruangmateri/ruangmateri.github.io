7. Buat analytical exposition text tentang why people should exercise


Jawaban:

A lot of us have claim that we do not have any time for exercise. We think that we are to busy and that exercise is not important and boring. But, many expert have said that exercise has a great role in building our body to be healthy .

Being physically active need a lot of advantage. Aside from that, exercise can reduce our weight, increase our height, and our body will become way fitter. Exercise also reduce our stress levels, improve our sleep patterns, and reduce the risk for us to get heart disease, diabetes and some forms of cancer or illness.

Exercise also brings us good effects in mentally. Because of the fitter body, exercise can make us feel more refreshed or happy and then we can increase our life by quality or expectancy.

Exercise can also be done in simple ways. We can go walking while shopping, we can take stair rather than lift to run up and down, and go cycling while enjoy the moment.

Over all, doing exercises will help us feel much better. In physically or mentally.

Jawaban:

1. What is global warming?

Global warming is the gradual increase in the overall temperature of earth's atmosphere.

2.Is it a severe problem? Why?

Yes, it is. It is a severe problem because global warming can make big holes in the ozone layers and cause the earth's temperature hotter than ever before. The hotter the earth's temperature, the more ice will melt in the poles and more lands and islands will get drowned. It can also cause extreme weather change, just like the ones we experience nowadays.

3. Translate the paragraph to Indonesian language.​

a gradual increase in the overall temperature of the earth's atmosphere generally attributed to the greenhouse effect caused by increased levels of carbon dioxide, chlorofluorocarbons, and other pollutants.

1. Apa itu pemanasan global?

Pemanasan global adalah peningkatan bertahap dalam suhu keseluruhan atmosfer bumi.

2. 2. Apakah ini masalah yang parah? Mengapa?

Ya itu. Ini masalah yang parah karena pemanasan global dapat membuat lubang besar di lapisan ozon dan menyebabkan suhu bumi lebih panas dari sebelumnya. Semakin panas suhu bumi, semakin banyak es yang mencair di kutub dan semakin banyak daratan dan pulau yang tenggelam. Hal ini juga dapat menyebabkan perubahan cuaca yang ekstrim, seperti yang kita alami saat ini.

3. Pemanasan Global adalah peningkatan bertahap suhu keseluruhan atmosfer bumi yang umumnya dikaitkan dengan efek rumah kaca yang disebabkan oleh peningkatan kadar karbon dioksida, klorofluorokarbon, dan polutan lainnya.
