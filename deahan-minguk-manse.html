= 2 : 3

= 12 : 18

P.sebenarnya kebun 1

= 12×350 = 4200 cm = 42 m

P.sebenarnya kebun 2

= 18×350 = 6300 cm = 63 m

→ l. Kebun 1 : l. Kebun 2

= 4 : 3

= 8 : 6

= 6×350 = 2100 cm = 21 m

→ L. Kebun 2 = 63×21 = 1.323 m²

Selisih = 1.323-1.176 = 147 m²
