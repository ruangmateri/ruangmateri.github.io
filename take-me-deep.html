Besides, I [2. be]to the toilet many times.jawablah dengan mengubah kata didalam kurung dengan present perfect tense atau past tense penjelasannya juga yah.​


Penjelasan:

Dian : Be optimistic. You just need to take a deep breath and stay focused.

Raka : I have taken a deep breath, but I still feel a bit nervous. Besides, I am.

Explanation:

"Take" is changed to "have taken" in the present perfect tense to indicate that the action of taking a deep breath has been completed and is still relevant in the present moment.

"Be" is left unchanged as it is already in the present tense, indicating the current state of being nervous.
