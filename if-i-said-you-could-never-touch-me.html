31. lengkapi kalimat berikut polite expression 1.May I........2.could/would I......3.could/would you.....4.I wonder If you could.....5.excuse me,please.....I could....6.excuse me,please.....I would.....7.would/could you mind.......8.Do you mind if.....9.If I were you.......


Subject : English

Category : Speaking

Sub-category : Polite request

Level : JHS

Code : 5

--------

Sometimes we need to ask for something or make a request. In order to not sound rude, we can make the request with some polite expressions.

-----

1. May I have your phone number, please?

2. Could/would I borrow your eraser ?

3. Could/would you open the door for me ?

4. I wonder If you could help me carry these books.

5. Excuse me, please help me. I could use a hand.

6. Excuse me, please turn on the AC. I would be very grateful.

7. Would/could you mind typing this letter for me ?

8. Do you mind if I leave early tomorrow ?

9. If I were you, I would call the police.
