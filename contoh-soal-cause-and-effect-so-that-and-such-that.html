5. Buat soal pilihan ganda so that and such that 10 soal


Explanation:

Such … that is followed by a noun phrase (can be singular or plural).

For example, She is such a genius girl that she always wins a science olympiad.

So … that is followed by an adjective or adverb or determiner (such as many, much, and little).

For example, your hair is so wonderful that everyone feels jealous.

18. 1. Jelaskan perbedaan penggunaan cause and effect pada conjunction so,...that dan such,..that secara singkat​


Jawaban:

Bedanya hanya terletak pada penempatan Part of Speech. Setelah kata such diikuti noun phrase (kata benda), sedangkan setelah kata so diikuti adjective (kata sifat).

Penjelasan:

Perhatikan dua contoh kalimat berikut ini:

That was such an amazing car that amazed everybody.The car was so amazing that everybody felt stunned.

Nah, dari dua kalimat di atas kita bisa tarik kesimpulan bahwa: such ... that dan so ... that digunakan untuk menyampaikan sebab akibat.

Seperti dari jawaban diatas, bahwa bedanya adalah hanya terletak pada penempatan Part of Speech. Setelah kata such diikuti noun phrase (kata benda), sedangkan setelah kata so diikuti adjective (kata sifat).

Jika dibuat rumus sederhana, maka akan menjadi seperti di bawah ini:

Such + Noun Phrase + ThatSo + Adjective + That

Contoh kalimat menggunakan Such ... That:

He is such a good boy that we always miss him.Siti is such a smart girl that she always wins a competition.

Contoh kalimat menggunakan So ... That:

The rain was so heavy that I was afraid to go out.The man was so scared that he worried to watch the movie.

Untuk lebih jelasnya silahkan perhatikan penjelasan di bawah ini tetntang perbedaan Such ... that dan So ... that.

• Such ... That

Also expresses a cause and effect.

Grammar:

such + (adjective) + NOUN + that

Examples:

Cause: It was a great movie.

Effect: I watched it several times.

It's such a great movie that I've watched it several times.

(great-adjective, movie-noun)

Cause: She is a very charming woman.

Effect: Everybody stares at her.

She is such a charming woman that everybody stares at her.

(charming-adjective, woman-noun)

• So...That

It expresses a cause and effect.

Grammar:

So + adjective/adverb + that

I was so sleepy that I couldn't keep my eyes open.

Subject Exercises:

So ... That / Such ... That ExerciseSo vs Such vs Such ASo vs Such vs Such A 2

Similar Exercises:

As ... As / Such ... thatSo ... that / Such ... as

Examples:

Cause: It was too windy.

Effect: We couldn't go sailing.

It was so windy that we couldn't go sailing.

(windy-adjective)

Cause: My sister is very shy.

Effect: She hides behind my mother when there are strangers around.

My sister is so shy that she hides behind my mother when there are strangers around.

(shy- adjective)

Cause: The dress was wonderfully designed.

Effect: I couldn't take my eyes off it.

The dress was so wonderfully designed that I couldn't take my eyes off it.

(wonderfully- adverb)

Note: such a very charming... "very" is dropped.

Attention

So...that is used with quantifiers (many, much, few, little) even if there is a noun.

Examples:

I have so many friends that I never get bored.He has so much money that he can buy whatever he wants.

32. contoh kalimat so that cause effect sentence berserta arti nya dan kalimat such that​


Jawaban:

1. **So that cause-effect sentence**:

Contoh kalimat: She studied diligently so that she could pass the exam.

Artinya: Dia belajar dengan tekun agar dia bisa lulus ujian.

Penjelasan: Kalimat tersebut menyatakan bahwa seseorang belajar dengan tekun (sebab) untuk mencapai tujuan atau akibat tertentu, yaitu lulus ujian.

2. **Such that sentence**:

Contoh kalimat: The instructions were clear such that everyone could understand them.

Artinya: Instruksinya jelas sehingga semua orang bisa mengerti.

Penjelasan: Kalimat ini menunjukkan bahwa tingkat kejelasan instruksi (kualitas instruksi) membuat semua orang dapat memahaminya. "Such that" digunakan untuk menunjukkan efek yang dihasilkan karena sifat atau kondisi tertentu.

36. Tolong dibantu Pengertian Cause and Effect: Now that,since so dan such that dan berikan contoh ke3nya..


Pengertian Cause and Effect: Now that,since so dan such that dan berikan contoh ke3nya..

JAWAB:

Pengertian Cause and Effect

cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

1. Now that:

Now .. that adalah conjuction yang di gunakan untuk menunjukkan terjadinya sesuatu yang disebabkan oleh keadaan seseorang atau benda.

Formula:

now + subject + verb/adverb + that

contoh:

Now Ammar lives far from his family that he can be an independent boyNow Amira is sick that she can't go to school.Now Iqbal has a new car that he can give a lift for his friends

2. Since so :

Since .. so adalah conjuction yang di gunakan untuk menunjukkan terjadinya sesuatu yang disebabkan oleh keadaan seseorang atau benda.

Formula:

since +subject + verb/adverb + so

contoh

Since Ammar has much money so he can buy a new car for his parents Since Ammira becomes a very famous singer so she is very busy every day Since Iqbal has been promoted as a manager so he always does his best in everything.

3. Such that :

Such ... that adalah conjuction yang di gunakan untuk menunjukkan terjadinya sesuatu yang disebabkan oleh kualitas seseorang atau benda.

Formula:

Such + noun + that

Ammar is such a very smart student that he can pass the test to enter in UI.Ammira has such a very beautiful voice that she becomes a very famous singerIqbal is such a good man that I choose him as my husband.

PEMBAHASAN:

Selain dari conjuction diatas ada beberapa conjuction for cause and effect lainnya, diantaranya adalah because, so, as, dan since

Namun mungkin ada yang belum paham apa yang dimaksud dengan conjuction, jadi saya akan menjelaskan sedikit mengenai conjuction. Conjunction adalah kata yang yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan 2 kata, frase atau kalimat menjadi 1 gabungan kalimat dengan pemaknaan tertentu. Ada banyak jenis conjunction yang perlu diketahui ketika belajar grammar dan structure. Namun kali ini kita hanya melihat connjuction yang dipakai untuk menghubungkan 2 kalimat yang menghasilakn arti sebab akibat (cause and effect relationship)

Conjunction for cause and effect yang paling sering dipakai adalah: because, so, as, dan since

Because artinya sebab sedangkan so mempunayi padanan arti dengan maka. Contoh penggunaanya dalam kalimat adalahs ebagai berikut:

I don’t go to school because I am sick I am sleepy, so I don’t go to school He can not drive as he does not have license Since he was sick, the teacher allowed her to stay at home

Secara umum ke 4 conjunction for cause and effect diatas mempunyai arti yang sama. Meski begitu dalam pembelajaran grammar tingakt lanjut, ada sedikit perbedaan dalam pemakaiannya.

Because digunakan karena merujuk pada alasan yang sangat penting dan belom diketahui ole yang lain As digunakan jika alasan yang disampaikan sudah diketahui Since digunakan sama seperti AS namun lebih formal

**Lisa**: Hi, Tom. How are you?

**Tom**: Do you find it difficult?

**Lisa**: Well, some parts are easy, but some parts are hard.

**Lisa**: I have trouble with the cause and effect sentences. You know, the ones that use so... that or such... that.

**Lisa**: Yes, please. That would be very helpful.

**Tom**: Okay, so we use so... that to show the result of an action or situation. For example, "He was so tired that he fell asleep on the couch." This means that he fell asleep on the couch because he was very tired.

**Lisa**: I see. So the first part is the cause and the second part is the effect, right?

**Tom**: Yes, exactly. And we use such... that to show the result of a quality or condition. For example, "It was such a beautiful day that we decided to go to the park." This means that we decided to go to the park because it was a very beautiful day.

**Lisa**: I get it. So the first part is the quality or condition and the second part is the effect, right?

**Tom**: Yes, you got it. Do you understand now?

**Lisa**: Yes, I think so. Thank you so much for explaining it to me.

**Tom**: You're welcome. I'm glad I could help.
