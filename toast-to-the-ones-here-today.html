11. how to make toast beserta stepsnya


ingridient:

3 large eggs
1 cup half-and-half
Pinch of salt
1 tablespoon of sugar
8 slices of bread
2 to 3 tablespoons butter, plus more for serving
Toppings (see Recipe Notes)

equipment:

Measuring cups and spoons
8x8-inch cake pan, or other pan that will hold 4 slices of bread
Whisk or fork
12- to 14-inch skillet
Spatula
Serving platter

instruction:

1. whisk the egg, salt, and sugar. in the cake pan until the egg is completely incorporated into the liquid.

2. Place the frying pan on the stove over medium-low heat. Move the pan with the custard next to the stove.

3. Place four slices of bread into the custard to soak for one minute. Flip the slices and soak another minute.

4. While the bread is soaking, add 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan. When it starts to foam, tilt the pan to completely coat the bottom. Lift one piece of bread from the custard and hold it briefly over the custard to let any excess drip off. Lay the bread gently in the frying pan. Repeat with additional pieces.

5. after a few minutes, peek under a slice of bread to see if the French toast has turned golden and browned. When it has, flip each piece and continue cooking. Monitor and adjust the heat so the toast doesn't burn.

6. When the first batch has nearly finished cooking, place the remaining four slices of bread in the custard to soak, one minute per side.

7. As the first batch of French toast finishes, transfer the pieces to a serving platter. Wipe out the frying pan. Return the pan to the flame, add 1 tablespoon of butter and cook the second batch of French toast.

15. How to make cheese toast


1.Prepare Teflon, rub a little margarine, let it melt.

2.Take 2 pieces of white bread, bake briefly on Teflon until crispy. Turn back and forth until cooked on both sides. Cut the cheese thinly and place it on bread, sprinkle sugar. Cover with another sheet of bread. Cook for a while so the cheese is hot and can melt.

3.Lift the toasted bread filled with melted cheese and ready to eat while warm.

39. The surfboards used twenty-five years ago were (more heavier) than the ones used by surfers todayToefl structure


The surfboards used twenty-five years ago were (heavier) than the ones used by surfers today



Heavier, karena dia pengecualian.
seperti Happy yang jadi Happier bukan more happy.

Rumus normalnya :

1 syllable + er

>1 syllable = more + (the words)


1 syllable contohnya "'hot" = 1 syllable jadi Hotter (lebih panas)

lebih dari 1 syllable  contohnya "expensive" (ex-pen-sive) = 3 syllables yang menjadi More Expensive.

Tapi untuk belakang "Y" seperti happy dan heavy kan 2 syllables "hap-py" dan "hea-vy" menjadi pengecualian.
mereka tidak di tulis "More happy" di lebih senangnya, tetapi happier.
begitu juga heavy, jadi Heavier bukan More Heavy.
