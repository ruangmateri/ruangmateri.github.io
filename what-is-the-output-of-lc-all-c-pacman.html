7. Apa output dari: LC_ALL=C pacman -V|tail -n3|base32|head -1 ? *


PENJELASAN

Perintah `LC_ALL=C pacman -V` digunakan untuk melihat versi dari paket pengelola paket Arch Linux, yang disebut `pacman`. Opsi `-V` memungkinkan Anda untuk melihat versi `pacman`.

Perintah `|tail -n3` mengarahkan output dari perintah sebelumnya ke `tail` dengan opsi `-n3`, yang berarti kita akan mendapatkan 3 baris terakhir dari output.

Perintah `|base32` mengarahkan output dari perintah sebelumnya ke `base32`, yang akan mengkodekan output menjadi basis 32.

Terakhir, perintah `|head -1` mengarahkan output dari perintah sebelumnya ke `head` dengan opsi `-1`, yang berarti kita hanya akan mendapatkan 1 baris pertama dari output tersebut.

Jadi, output dari perintah yang diberikan adalah sebuah string yang telah dikodekan ke dalam basis 32.

8. An electric motor in a washing machine has a power output of 1 kW. work done in 20 min. In a tug of war A-team is leading B-team. The rope is moving tow team at a regular rate of 0.01 ms. If the tension of the rope is 4000 N W power output of A-team? The rate of doing work for the first worker is twice that of the second w the working hours per day of the second is two and a half times that o Who is a better worker? A system of levers with a velocity ratio of 25 overcomes a resistance when an effort of 165 N is applied to it, calculate (i) the mechanical a the system (ii) its efficiency. By using a block-and-tackle a man can raise a load of 720 N by an N. Find the mechanical advantage of the method. The length of an unstretched spring is 12.0 cm. What load is nee spring to a length of 15 cm? The spring constant is 100 Nm. 23.47 ✔​


Let's break down the questions one by one:

1. **Electric motor in a washing machine:**

Power output = 1 kW

Time = 20 min = 20/60 hours = 1/3 hours

To find the work done (in Joules):

Work = Power × Time

Work = 1 kW × (1/3) hours = 1000 W × (1/3) hours = 333.33 Joules

2. **Tug of war:**

Rate of movement of the rope (velocity) = 0.01 m/s

Tension in the rope = 4000 N

To find the power output of A-team:

Power = Force × Velocity

Power = 4000 N × 0.01 m/s = 40 W

3. **Comparing workers:**

Let's assume the work done by the first worker is W Joules, and the work done by the second worker is 0.5W Joules (since the first worker's rate is twice that of the second worker).

Let the working hours per day of the second worker be D hours.

So, the working hours per day of the first worker = 2.5D hours.

Now, the total work done by each worker in a day is:

First worker: 2.5D × W Joules

Second worker: D × 0.5W Joules

Comparing the workers:

The first worker does more work:

2.5D × W > D × 0.5W

2.5 > 0.5

D > 0.5

Since D is greater than 0.5, the first worker is the better worker in terms of work done.

4. **System of levers:**

Velocity ratio of the system of levers = 25

Effort applied = 165 N

To find the mechanical advantage of the system:

Mechanical Advantage (MA) = Velocity Ratio = 25

To find the efficiency of the system:

Efficiency = (MA / VR) × 100%

Efficiency = (25 / (25 + 1)) × 100% = 96% (approx)

5. **Block-and-tackle:**

Load lifted by the man = 720 N

Effort applied by the man = N (Let's find the value of N)

To find the mechanical advantage of the block-and-tackle:

Mechanical Advantage (MA) = Load / Effort

720 N = MA × N

We are not given the number of ropes/pulleys in the block-and-tackle system, so we cannot determine the exact value of MA and N without that information.

6. **Unstretched spring:**

Length of the unstretched spring (L0) = 12.0 cm = 0.12 m

Length of the stretched spring (L) = 15 cm = 0.15 m

Spring constant (k) = 100 N/m

To find the load needed to stretch the spring to a length of 15 cm:

Hooke's Law: F = k × (ΔL)

ΔL = L - L0 = 0.15 m - 0.12 m = 0.03 m

Load (F) = 100 N/m × 0.03 m = 3 N

Pembahasan

Perintah LC_ALL=C pacman -V digunakan untuk menampilkan versi dari manajer paket Pacman pada sistem operasi Arch Linux atau distro yang menggunakan Pacman sebagai manajer paket default. Flag -V digunakan untuk menampilkan informasi versi.

Terakhir, perintah head -1 akan mengambil baris pertama dari keluaran hasil konversi basis-32 tersebut.
