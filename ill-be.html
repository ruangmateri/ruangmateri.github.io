35. Rina was ill yesterday (+)​


Jawaban:

(+) Rina was ill yesterday.

(-) Rina wasn't ill yesterday.

(?) Was Rina ill yesterday?

Simple Past Tense (Nominal)

Simple past tense nominal digunakan untuk keadaan di masa lampau (past) tetapi tidak ada verb (kata kerja), jadi menggunakan To be/Verb be yaitu Was/Were.

Formula :

Subject + Was/Were + Adjective/Adverb/Noun + Time Signal
