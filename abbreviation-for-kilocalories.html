21. What's the difference between acronym and abbreviation (and give an example) ? Biar lebih jelas, tolong dijelaskan juga dalam bahasa Indonesia


The difference between an acronym and an abbreviation is a matter of pronunciation. Both of these terms are short ways to write a longer phrase, and they are both considered to be abbreviations. However, acronyms are a subset of abbreviations, and they only earn that title when they are pronounced as a word.

Example —

Abbreviations:

• NSA (National Security Agency)

• IRS (Internal Revenue Service)

• FBI (Federal Bureau of Investigation)

• USA (United States of America)

• UK (United Kingdom)

Acronyms:

• UNICEF: United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund”; now: “United Nations Children’s Fund”

• NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration)

• RADAR “RAdio Detection And Ranging” or “RAdio Direction And Ranging”/RADAR; now: “radar”)

etc.

Penjelasan

Jadi Abbrev sama Acronym itu sebenarnya sama. Yaitu menyingkat long phrases gitu. Tapi beda diantara keduanya itu dari pronounciation. Bagaimana kita mengatakan singkatan itu.

Kalau Abbreviation, kita menyebut singkatannya per-huruf. Contoh terlampir, United Kingdom disingkat UK. Maka kita baca "Yu Kei". United State of America kita maca "Yu Es Ei".

Kalau Acronym, kita baca langsung semua huruf. Contoh terlampir, NASA. Kita baca biasa saja yaitu " NASA". Tidak "En A Es A"

Penjelasan:

1. A.m. atau ante merediem digunakan untuk menunjukkan waktu antara pukul 00.00 sampai pukul 12.00 (tengah malam) dan p.m. atau post merediem digunakan untuk menjelaskan waktu antara 12.00 (siang) sampai 00.00.

2, 3, 4:

Seperti yang kamu tahu bahwa di dalam jam terdapat angka 1-12. Jika jarum panjang berada di angka 12 kebawah kamu menambahkan ... past....

Contoh: 06.25 -> Twenty five past six

Namun, jika jarum berada di angka 6 hanya menambahkan half to.

Contoh: 06.30 -> Half past six

Lalu, jika jarum panjang berada di atas 6, maka harus menambahkan ... to ....

Contoh: 06.41 -> Nine teen to Ten

Kenapa bisa begitu? Biasanya saat menunjukkan pukul tersebut kita selalu bilang 7 kurang sembilan belas, 4 kurang sepuluh (03.50), dan lain-lain. Caranya untuk menunjukkan pukul diatas, rumusnya 60-41 = 19

27. what is the difference between abbreviation and acronym give the examples


An abbreviation and an acronym are both shortened versions of something else. Both can often be represented as a series of letters.

Abbreviation vs. Acronym

There is a great deal of overlap between abbreviations and acronyms. Every acronym is an abbreviation because the acronym is a shortened form of a word or phrase. However, not every abbreviation is an acronym, since some abbreviations - those made from words - are not new words formed from the first few letters of a series of words.

Abbreviation

An abbreviation is a shortened form of a word or phrase, as N.Y. for New York, Mr. for Mister, lb for pound or ctn for carton.

There are millions of common abbreviations used every day.

When you write out your address, most people write "St. or Ave." instead of "street" or "avenue."

Acronym

An acronym, technically, must spell out another word. However, this rule isn't always rigidly enforced:

ASAP is an acronym although the word "asap" is not in many dictionaries. Still, the first letters of each of the words "As Soon As Possible" are used to form the acronym ASAP.

Abbreviation consists of 2 forms, as follows:

1. Shortening

Shortening is a form of abbreviation where the initial and final letters are eliminated.

Example:

The flu comes from the word Influenza: - I get influenza now (I have the flu now).

St comes from the word street: -we can meet in that street in the morning (we can meet on that street in the morning).

Ad comes from the word advertisement: - I saw a beautiful advertisement there (I saw a beautiful advertisement there).

2. Initialism

Namely the form of abbreviations by taking the initial letters in each word so as to form a new word.

Example:

UK (united Kingdom): -I live in the UK now (I live in the UK now).

FBI (Federal Bureau of Investigation): -I am the member of the FBI (I am a member of the FBI).
