22. fungsi I WOULD LIKE TO, I WILL, I AM GOING TO and WOULD RATHER


I would like to... = aku mau... (sopan, jadi biasanya digunakan untuk percakapan formal)
contoh:
A: "Sir, what would you like to do?"
B: "I would like to eat breakfast first."

I will... = akan (tapi ini spontan, belum direncanakan)
contoh:
A: "Your cat's gone!"
B: "What? I will look for it!"

I'm going to... = akan (tapi sudah direncakan akan ngapain sebelumnya)
contoh:
A: "Where are you going?"
B: "I'm going to the minimarket."

Would rather... = lebih baik
contoh:
A: "I'd rather stay with you than go with them."
B: "I know. I'd rather do the same thing."

34. MY SISTER AND I ..... GOING TO JAKARTA  YESTERDAY​


Jawaban:

My sister and I were going to Jakarta yesterday.

Penjelasan:

PLURAL adalah jamak/lebih dari satu.

SINGULAR adalah tunggal/hanya satu.

Was dipake untuk first person singular dan third person singular.

yaitu; I, he, she, it.

Were dipake untuk second person singular dan plural, lalu first dan third person plural.

yaitu; you, your, yours, we, they.

jadii, itu kan my sister and I berarti kami (we) nah makanya pakai Were.

40. Contoh dialog bahasa Inggris i am going to and i would like to


Here is the example of dialogue using going to and would like to.

W: Dude, I heard you're going to change your major at university, is it true?

Y: Yeah, Wamberto, I don't think I can't take it anymore, dealing with chemical engineering stuff.

W: So, you just give up the whole year here?

Y: I'm afraid so. I feel lost here, my passion is not here. I'm going to take another entrance test for English Literature.

W: Really? You switch to social subject? C'mon, dude, think it over. Think of your parents.

Y: Well, I have told them. I just wanna live with my passion. I would like to be a writer or screenwriter.

W: OK, I wish you luck, Yuri.

Y: Thanks, mate. I'll pay a visit sometime later.

Penjelasan:

Frase 'going to' dan 'would like to' memiliki arti dan fungsi yang berbeda satu sama lain.

'Going to' artinya adalah 'akan' dan berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan rencana untuk melakukan sesuatu di suatu waktu yang akan datang.'Would like to' artinya adalah 'ingin' dan berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan ambisi atau keinginan.
