The sentence "Rani and Sinta are my classmates" means that both Rani and Sinta are students who attend the same class or educational institution as the speaker. They share the same learning environment or classroom with the speaker. This sentence is used to describe a relationship or association between the speaker and their fellow students, Rani and Sinta.

Jawaban:

they are my new classmates in this semester.

Penjelasan:

be/ to be terdiri atas am, is, are

to be - am digunakan untuk subjek I

to be - is digunakan untuk subjek He (dia laki-laki), She (dia perempuan), nama orang (Andi, Budi, Sari, Dian, dsb), kepunyaan ( my father, my mother, my sister, my brother, my grandfather, etc.)

to be - are digunakan untuk subjek you, they.
