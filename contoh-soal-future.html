Rumus Simple Future Tense
1. Positif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will go to school soon
The students are going to have a test next month
2. Negatif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + not + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will not go to school soon
The students are not going to have a test next
3. Pertanyaan
Will/shall + subject + verb I + object
Am/is/are + subject + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
Will we go to school soon ?
Are the students going to have a test next month ?Latihan Soal Simple Future TenseBerikut latihan Simple Future tense bahasa inggris. 4. apa rumus dari simple future dan future continous. beserta contoh!


simple future tense :
. Verbal Sentences :
 (+) S+Will+V1+o (-) S+Will+not+V1+o (?) Will+S+V1+o Example : (+)  She will meet me tomorrow.
 (-)  She will’ not meet me tomorrow
(?)  Will she meet me tomorrow ? 
future continus tense :
Formula’s : (+) S + will + be + v-ing+ O
(-) S + will not +be + V- ing + O
(?) Will + S+ be + V-ing + O Example : (+)  He will be teaching in my house tomorrow. (-)  He will not be teaching in my house tomorrow (?)  Will he be teaching in my house tomorrow ?

10. contoh 5 kalimat simple future. future continues. dan future perfect


Simple future tense
1. I will go to Bandung tomorrow
2. She will eat meatball next sunday
3. my mother will cook mushroom soup today
4. the students will go to museum tomorrow
5 they will play badminton next week

Simple Future :

S + will + V1

- Examples :

1. I will cut my hair tomorrow

2. She will go to the market next week

3. He will buy a bicycle tomorrow

4. They will watch a movie tomorrow

5. We will take a test next Tuesday


Future Continous :

S + will + be + Ving

- Examples :

1. I will be cutting my hair in the afternoon

2. She will be going to the market this time next week

3. He will be buying a bicycle this time tomorrow

4. They will be watching a movie tonight

5. We will be taking a test this time next Tuesday


Future Perfect :

S + will + have + V3

-Examples :

1. I will have cut my hair

2. She will have gone to the market

3. He will have bought a bicycle

4. They will have watched a movie

5. We will have taken a test

19. Contoh kalimat simple future, future continues, future perfect verbal dan nominal


1. Simple Future
a) Verbal: I will watch TV tomorrow.
b) Nominal: I will be at home tomorrow.

2. Future Continuous
a) Verbal: I will be watching TV tomorrow at 07.00 a.m.
b) Nominal: I will be at home tomorrow at 07.00 a.m.

3. Future Perfect
a) Verbal: I will have watched TV by tomorrow.
b) Nominal: I will have been at home by tomorrow.

24. Buat fungsi simple past tense dan future tense, rumusnya ,contoh kalimatnya (+, _, ?) Dan contoh soal pilihan ganda 4 (2 past tense, 2 future tense)​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa lampau

future tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

rumus:

simple past

+ ) s + v2+ O.

-) s + did + v1 + O

?) did + s +v1 + O ?.

simple future

+ ) s + will/shall +v1 + O

- ) s + will/shall+not+v1+O

? ) will/shall+s+v1+O ?.

contoh :

simple past tense

+ ) i ate rice , yesterday( saya makan nasi kemarin)

-) i did eat rice, yesterday(saya tidak makan nasi kemarin)

? ) did i eat rice,yesterday ?.(apakah saya makan nasi kemarin?)

simple future

+ ) i will eat rice tomorrow ( aku akan makan nasi besok)

- ) i wil not eat rice tomorrow(aku tidak akan makan nasi besok)

? ) will i eat rice tomorrow ?.(akankan aku makan nasi besok?)

note: untuk penggunaan "will" ketika subjek kalimat berbentuk (i,you,we,they,she,he,it,dan nama orang lebih dari satu nama ex: doni and riza)

untuk penggunaan shall (I,she,he,it,dan nama orang tunggal ex : doni )
