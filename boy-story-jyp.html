16. tolong bantu saya menjawab pertanyaan ini.1. what is a more suitable title for the previous story?2. who were involved in the story?3. why did the countryman consider the man and his son fools?4. why did the man ask the boy to ride on the donkey?5. is it true that boy let his father walk while he rode?6. what is the main purpose of the story?​


Reading Comprehension

1. What is a more suitable title for the previous story?

(Apa judul yang lebih sesuai dengan cerita sebelumnya?)

"Be Wise to Make Decision"

("Bijaklah untuk membuat keputusan")

2. Who were involved in the story?

(Siap yang terlibat dalam cerita?)

A man, his son, donkey, countrymen and countrywomen.

(Seorang lelaki, anaknya, keledai, orang-orang di nergerinya)

3. Why did the countryman consider the man and his son fools?

(Mengapa seseorang di nergerinya menganggap lelaki dan anaknya itu bodoh?)

Because the man let the donkey walk without a load on its back.

(Karena laki-laki tersebut membiarkan keledai berjalan tanpa muatan di punggungnya)

4. Why did the man ask the boy to ride on the donkey?

(Mengapa laki-laki meminta anaknya mengendarai keledai?)

Because he thought that the donkey was to ride on, after being told so by the countryman.

(Karena dia berpikir bahwa keledai itu untuk dikendarai, setelah diberitahu demikian oleh seseorang di negerinya)

5. Is it true that boy let his father walk while he rode?

(Apakah benar bahwa anak laki-laki tersebut membiarkan ayahnya berjalan sementara dia naik keledai?)

Yes, it is true.

(Ya, benar)

6. What is the main purpose of the story?​

(Apa tujuan utama cerita tersebut?)

To tell us that we can't please anyone if we please all. We shouldn't take all the advice right away. We should be wise to hear and choose the advice from people around us.

(Untuk memberitahu kita bahwa kita tidak dapat membuat siapapun senang jika kita menuruti semua keinginan orang-orang. Kita tidak boleh mengambil semua nasihat begitu saja. Kita harus bijak untuk mendengar dan memilih nasihat dari orang-orang di sekitar kita)

32. Change this story into passive voice.Yesterday acarhit a boy onthestreet. Awitness called an ambulanceand the emergency service took the boy to the hospital. Two doctors did a surgery to the boy. Finally, the local police arrested the unobeyed driver


Answer:

Yesterday, a boy was hit by a car on the street. An ambulance was called by a witness and the boy was taken by the emergency service to the hospital. The boy was done a surgery by two doctors. Finally, the unobeyed driver was arrested by the local police.

-> jawaban dicetak tebal merupakan perubahan dari penyampaian aktif ke pasif

Discussion:

Kalimat pasif adalah sebuah kumpulan kata dengan mendahului obyek bertukar tempat menjadi subyek dan sebaliknya, obyek berperan sebagai sesuatu diperlakukan oleh subyek. Pada kalimat pasif, terdapat perubahan dari kalimat aktifnya.

Agar dapat merubah kalimat aktif ke pasif, perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat:

perubahan verbaadanya penggunaan to beobyek mendahului subyek (obyek + verba + subyek)O + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + by — S + ...dengan to be diperhatikan dari obyeknya (is: he, she, it, name, object | am: I | are: you, they, we, names, objects)verba dari simple present diubah menjadi bentuk verba untuk pasif (past participle (V3))titik-titik dapat diisi sebagai sisa kalimat

Kalimat pada soal berupa bentuk simple past—umumnya teks naratif mesti diceritakan dalam pengungkapan peristiwa di masa lalu.

O + to be (was/were) + V3 + by — S + ...to be diperhatikan dari obyeknya (was: I, he, she, it, name, object | were: you, they, we, names, objects)

Explanation:

Sekarang kembali pada soal; untuk merubah kalimat aktif ke pasif dapat menjadi:

-> Yesterday (C) a car (S) hit (V2) a boy (O) on the street (C)

menjadi:

Yesterday, a boy (O) was hit (was + V3) by a car (S) on the street.

-> A witness (S) called (V2) an ambulance (O) and the emergency service (S) took (V2) the boy (O) to the hospital (C)

(terdapat kata hubung and menggabungkan kalimat terpisah)

menjadi:

An ambulance (O) was called (was + V3) by a witness (S) and the boy (O) was taken (was + V3) by the emergency service (S) to the hospital.

-> Two doctors (S) did (V2) a surgery to the boy (O)

(obyeknya adalah the boy bukan a surgery, kata itu merujuk ke sesuatu dikerjakan oleh subyek)

menjadi:

The boy was done a surgery by two doctors.

-> Finally, the local police (S) arrested (V2) the unobeyed driver (O)

menjadi:

Finally, the unobeyed driver was arrested by the local police.

Hope this answer could help and please accept the apologies if there was any mistake.
