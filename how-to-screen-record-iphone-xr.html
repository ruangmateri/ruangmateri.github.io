37. P S T R Pada layang-layang PQRS, PR = 25 cm, SQ = 24 cmdan RT = 16 cm. Keliling PQRS adalah ... Click to add notes 22 Participants Chat Share Screen Record Leave Reactions Hi


Jawaban:

70 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

diket :

layang layang PQRS

PR = 25 cm

SQ = 24 cm

RT = 16 cm

ditanya :

Keliling PQRS...?

jawab :

cari panjang PQ dan QR :

PQ = √PT²+QT²

PT = 25 - 16 = 9 cm

QT = 24 : 2 = 12

PQ = √9²+12²

= √81+144

= √225

= 15 cm

QR = √RT² + QT²

RT = 16 cm

QT = 12 cm

QR = √16²+12²

= √256+144

= √400

= 20 cm

cari keliling PQRS :

K =2×(PQ+QR)

= 2 × (15+20)

= 2×35

= 70 cm
