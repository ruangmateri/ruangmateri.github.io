Dialogue A

Mommy : Joko, breakfast is (1) ready.

Joko : Yes mom, wait.

Mommy: Mommy (2) cooks your favorite dish.

Joko : Wow, chicken fried rice, (3) thank you, mom.

Mommy: Yes my dear, enjoy your food.

(Untuk jawaban nomor 2 hanya perkiraan. Pada kata rumpang nomor 2, diisikan dengan kata kerja).

(Alter oating)

Joko : I will go to school mom, (4) see you.

Mommy: Okay boy, (5) see you and good luck for your study, good-bye.

(jawaban nomor 4 dan 5 juga hanya perkiraan. Yang diisikan adalah leave-taking expression)

Dialogue B

Mr. Sihombing : Where are you going, Kadek?

Kadek : No, I am not Mr. Sihombing. I want to (3) meet my friend, first.

Mr. Sihombing : I see. Be careful Kadek.

Mr. Sihombing: (5) See you, Kadek.

Activity 3. Read the dialogue below to answer the questions that follow!

Winda : Mommy, there will be a meeting in the school after class around 3 P.M., may go there with Wulan by riding motorcycle?

Mommy : Yes, you may go but it is better for you to take a walk because the school is not far from our home. I amworried if you ride the motorcycle.

Winda: But, mom ...

Mommy : You may use motorcycle when you are in senior high school.

Winda : Yes, Mommy​.

1. What is the expression used in the dialog above?

Expression of asking permission

2. How does Winda ask for permission?

May go there with Wulan by riding motorcycle?

3. Does Mommy permit Winda go by riding motorcycle?

No, she does not

4. When does Mommy permit Winda go to school by motorcycle?

When Winda is in senior high school

Pembahasan

1. What is the expression used in the dialog above?

Expression of asking permission

Penjelasan: Ungkapan yang digunakan adalah asking permission, ditunjukkan dengan kalimat may go there with Wulan by riding motorcycle?

2. How does Winda ask for permission?

May go there with Wulan by riding motorcycle?

Penjelasan: Winda menanyakan ijin dengan mengatakan "May go there with Wulan by riding motorcycle?"

3. Does Mommy permit Winda go by riding motorcycle?

No, she does not

Penjelasan: Ibunya tidak mengizinkan Winda naik motor dengan mengatakan "Yes, you may go but it is better for you to take a walk because the school is not far from our home"

4. When does Mommy permit Winda go to school by motorcycle?

When Winda is in senior high school

Penjelasan: Ibunya mengizinkan Winda pergi ke sekolah ketika dia sudha SMA, ditunjukkan dengan kalimat "You may use motorcycle when you are in senior high school."

Penggunaan asking-giving permission:

Can I + verb (informal)May I + verb (formal)Do you think I could + verb (formal)Would you mind if I + verb in past (formal)

Responses:

Sure (giving permission)No problem (giving permission)Unfortunately, I need to say no (refusing permission)I'm afraid that's not possible (refusing permission)
