apa arti dari be your self and never surrender​?

The expression of be yourself means that you have to be the best version of yourself at anytime, anywhere.

The expression of never surrender means that you have to give all your best effort to everything that you do, realistically.

Secara realistis maksudnya dengan cara yang menunjukkan ide yang masuk akal dan praktis tentang apa yang dapat dicapai atau diharapkan.
