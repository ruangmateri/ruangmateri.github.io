Penjelasan:

The general rule of thumb is to use one tea bag per cup of water. However, you can adjust this to taste. If you like a stronger cup of tea, you can use more tea bags, or steep the tea for longer. If you prefer a weaker cup of tea, you can use fewer tea bags, or steep the tea for a shorter time.

Here are some tips for making the perfect cup of tea:

1. Use fresh, cold water.

2. Bring the water to a boil, then remove it from the heat and let it cool for a minute or two.

3. Add the tea bags to the hot water and let them steep for the recommended amount of time. The steeping time will vary depending on the type of tea.

4. Remove the tea bags and enjoy your tea!

1. Memiliki kreasi yang unik dan langka karena tidak semua orang mampu melukiskan wajah berbagai jenis makhluk hidup di atas tempat minum melengkung seperti pada gambar di atas.
