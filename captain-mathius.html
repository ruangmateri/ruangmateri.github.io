Jawaban:( + ) the captain cancelled the voyage( - )the captain did not cancel the voyage( ? ) Did the captain cancel the voyage?

PEMBAHASAN

Kalimat Positive (+) pada soal menggunakan Verb-2 “cancelled” setelah subject “the captain”

maka kalimat tersebut Past Tense.

PAST TENSE adalah tenses untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu. Rumusnya:

(+) S + V2 + Complement

(-) S + did not + V1 + complement

(?) Did + S + V1 + complement?

32. skipper means captain​


Jawaban:

The word “skipper” originated from the Dutch word schipper, which means “a person who commands a ship” . A schipper leads his crew to their destination, or achieve some other goal. In sport, the captain of a team has the same job to fulfill. Hence, he is called “skipper”

Jawaban:

General Knowledge

A skipper is known as a captain of a boat or a ship.
