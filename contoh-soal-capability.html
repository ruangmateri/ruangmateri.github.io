9. Contoh dialog yang memuat willingness dan capability untuk 3 orang


Contoh dialog yang memuat willingness dan capability untuk 3 orang adalah sebagai berikut.


Anna: Hey, guys, I will need your help.

Lulu: What is it Anna?

Anna: So I decided to move from the house that I rented to an apartment. The problem is, I have so many things that I don’t know whether I will be able to pack and transport all of them by myself.

Ika: That’s a piece of cake! Don’t worry Anna, you got us. I will be very willing to help you pack your stuff. What about you Lulu?

Lulu: Sure, me too! I am able to do it as long as we do it together.

Anna: Of course yes! I wouldn’t be able to do it alone either. Thanks a lot guys, I love you all!

Lulu & Ika: We love you too, Ann!


Pembahasan

Will dan Won't adalah istilah yang paling banyak digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris untuk membicarakan tentang masa yang akan datang. Tetapi selain itu will dan won't memiliki fungsi yang lain, salah satunya untuk mengekspresikan keinginan atau ketidakinginan.


Expressing willingness, unwillingness:

will, won't, would, wouldn't


In the present: will, won’t

Kita dapat gunakan kata will untuk mengatakan bahwa seseorang ingin melakukan sesuatu:

Tom's a really kind person; he will always help you if you need somethingJosh will always be there to listen to your story, no matter how busy he is

Kita juga dapat gunakan kata will not/won't untuk mengatakan ketidakinginan untuk melakukan sesuatu:

David doesn't want to talk to me – he won't answer the phone.My car won't start - I'll have to take the bus

In the past: would, wouldn’t

Ketika berbicara dengan form lampau, kita dapat menggunakan would untuk menyatakan seseorang ingin melakukan sesuatu:

Tom was a really kind person; he'd (he would) always help anyone in any situation.Josh would love to come to your house on the weekend.

Menyatakan ketidakinginan untuk melakukan sesuatu di masa lampau:

My dad always helped with the cooking, but he wouldn't wash the dishes.I was late for work today because my car wouldn't start.

Ability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kemampuan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could (past) dan managed to. Berikut ini adalah formula untuk menyatakan ability dalam bentuk present tense:


1. Is/am/are able to + main verb

Thomas is able to play the piano.Katy is not able to play the piano.

2. Can + main verb

Katy can play the flute.Thomas can't play the flute.Pelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang willingness and ability:

wahyu: Good morning Hanna. (Selamat pagi Hanna)

Hanna: Are you busy today wahyu? (Apakah kamu sibuk hari ini wahyu?)

wahyu: Not really. Why? (Tidak juga. Kenapa?)

Hanna: I want to ask you a favor: (Aku ingin minta tolong padamu)

wahyu: What is it? (Apa itu?)

Hanna: Can you help me fix my laen mower? (Bisakah kamu membantuku memperbaiki mesin pemotong rumputku?)

wahyu: Yes, it’s okay. (Ya, tidak apa-apa.)

Hanna: Very well. Let’s fix it. (Baiklah. Ayo kita perbaiki.)

wahyu: Okay. (Oke.)

30. contoh make a dialogue about capability can​


Jawaban:

Here's an example of a dialogue about capability:

Alex: Hey Sarah, have you ever thought about what it means to have capability?

Sarah: Yeah, I think capability refers to the capacity or ability to do something effectively or efficiently.

Alex: Absolutely! It's about having the necessary skills, knowledge, and resources to accomplish tasks or achieve goals.

Sarah: Right. And I believe that everyone has their own unique set of capabilities. It's about recognizing and harnessing those strengths.

Alex: I agree. Our capabilities can vary in different areas, for example, someone may be really capable in solving math problems, while another person might excel in creative writing.

Sarah: That's true. Capability can be developed through experience, education, and continuous learning. It's not fixed; we can always improve and expand our capabilities.

Alex: Definitely. It's important to challenge ourselves and step out of our comfort zones to discover and enhance our potential.

Sarah: Absolutely, trying new things and learning from failures can help us grow and develop new capabilities.

Alex: Right. And it's also crucial to have a growth mindset, believing that our capabilities can be strengthened through effort and determination.

Sarah: I couldn't agree more. Having a positive mindset and believing in our own capabilities can lead to greater achievements and personal growth.

Alex: So true. Let's continue to challenge ourselves and push our boundaries to unlock our full potential and maximize our capabilities.

Sarah: Absolutely! Let's strive for continuous improvement and make the most of our capabilities in everything we do.

In this dialogue, Alex and Sarah discuss the meaning of capability and emphasize the importance of recognizing, developing, and utilizing one's unique strengths and abilities. They also highlight the significance of continuous learning, a growth mindset, and personal growth.
