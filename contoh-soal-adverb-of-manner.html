Penjelasan:

ADVERB OF MANNER

adalah keterangan cara memberi tahu kita bagaimana sesuatu terjadi. Keterangan cara biasanya diletakkan setelah kata kerja utama atau setelah objek. Contoh

He ran quickly.

She spoke softly.

He plays the flute beautifully. (setelah objek langsung)

He ate the chocolate cake greedily. (setelah objek langsung)

#Keterangan cara tidak dapat diletakkan di antara kata kerja dan objek langsungnya. Kata keterangan harus diletakkan sebelum kata kerja atau di akhir klausa. Contoh

He ate greedily the chocolate cake. [salah]

He ate the chocolate cake greedily. [benar]

He greedily ate the chocolate cake. [benar]

He gave us generously the money. [salah]

He gave us the money generously. [benar]

He generously gave us the money. [benar]

#Jika ada preposisi sebelum objek dari kata kerja, kita dapat meletakkan keterangan cara sebelum preposisi atau setelah objek. Contoh

The child ran happily towards his mother.

The child ran towards his mother happily.

#Keterangan cara harus selalu mengikuti kata kerja yang tidak memliki objek (kata kerja intransitif). Contoh

The town grew quickly after 1997.

He waited patiently for his mother to arrive.

#Keterangan-keterangan cara yang umum ini hampir selalu diletakkan tepat setelah kata kerja: well, badly, hard, dan fast. Contoh

He swam well despite being tired.

The rain fell hard during the storm.
