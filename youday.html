3. the students sing the national song loudly in the flang ceremony every monday simple present tense dan present continuous tense​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense: The students sing the national song loudly in the flag ceremony every Monday.

Present continuous tense: The students are singing the national song loudly in the flag ceremony every Monday.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas sudah berbentuk simple present tense (subject + verb 1).

Subjek (the students) berbentuk jamak, sehingga kata kerjanya tidak diikuti s/es.

Pola simple present tense untuk subjek jamak (I, you, they, we) yaitu

Subject + verb 1 (without s/es).

Pola present continuous tense untuk subjek jamak (I, you, they, we) yaitu

Subject + are + verb -ing.

Pelajari lebih lanjut contoh kalimat present continuous tense pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/38761182

28. (+) He studies English every Monday.(-) He (doesn't / not) study English every Monday.​


Jawaban:

(+) He studies English every Monday.

(-) He doesn't study English every Monday.

(?) Does he study English every Monday?

Penjelasan:

Kalimat jawaban di atas merupakan bentuk kalimat Verbal Simple Present Tense.

Rumus Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

- Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

- Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

- Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + ?

Semoga membantu ya.

37. (+)they find their file yoday ubahla ke bentuk(-&?)​


Jawaban:

- they do not find their file today

? do they find their file today?

Penjelasan:

jika (-) tambah not+do/does

jika (?) do/does didepan

Jawaban:

(-) they do not find their file today

(?) do they find their file today ?

Pembahasan

Structure Kalimat soal terdiri dari

Subject (they) + Verb 1 (find)Maka kalimat tersebut adalah Present Tense.

Present Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan kebenaran umum/fakta.

Berdasarkan Rumus:

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

they find their file today

(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O

they do not find their file today

(?) do/does + S + V1 + O?

do they find their file today ?

Catatan:

Menggunakan Auxiliary DO karena Subjek jamak yaitu THEY (mereka)
