40. 1. Every day, the farmer ... the roses. The plants ... well. a. Water - is taken care. b. Waters - are taken care. c. Are watering - blooming. d. Is watered - are smelling. e. Are watered - is blooming. 2. A corruptor is someone who ... people's right. Their pledge ... by their greed. a. Doesn't misuses - are overtaken. b. Doesn't misuse - is overtaken. c. Don't misuse - is overtaken. d. Misuses - is overtaken. e. Misuse - overtakes.


Jawaban:

1. B

2. D

Penjelasan:

1. Every day, the farmer waters the roses= ciri present tense adalah terdapat kata every day (setiap hari). Jadi verb bentuk pertama (water) harus digunakan dan kalimat ini memiliki subject 1 orang yang disebut singular.

The plants are taken care well=kalimat ini merupakan passive voice dalam bentuk present tense. Jadi verb ke tiga harus digunakan (take care jadi taken care). To be 'are' menunjukkan bahwa the plants merupakan subject lebih dari satu (plural).

2. A corruptor is someone who misuses people's right=bentuk kalimat present tense. Misuses merujuk ke subject singular/1 orang (someone)(present tense)

Their pledge is overtaken by their pledge. Kalimat ini merupakan passive voice dalam bentuk present tense. Jadi verb ke tiga harus digunakan (overtake care jadi overtaken). To be 'is' menunjukkan bahwa the pledge merupakan subject uncountable (tidak bisa dihitung) jadi dianggap singular.
