2. * d. pastmona - field - the - works - in - farmerThe good arrangement isa. a field works in the farmerb. a farmer works in the fieldc. the field works in a farmerd. a farmer in the works field​


Susunan yang tepat dari kata-kata tersebut adalah..

Answer :

A farmer works in the field. (Seorang petani bekerja di sawah/ladang)

Penjelasan

Dalam menyusun kalimat bahasa Inggris tidak jauh berbeda dengan bahasa Indonesia yaitu struktur nya harus SPOK atau kalau di bahasa Inggris di kenal dengan S + Verb + O

Struktur dari kalimat "A farmer works in the field" adalah..

A farmer (seorang petani) => Subject

Works (bekerja) => Verb1 + s, karena subject nya tunggal "a farmer"

In the field (di sawah) => Adverb of place

Karena menggunakan verb1 dan ada tambahan -s pada kata kerja nya, maka kalimat tersebut masuk ke dalam simple present tense, yang mana rumus nya :

S + V1 (s/es) + O

PEMBAHASAN

1.The farmer the rice plant the field grows in

=> The farmer grows the rice plant in the field

(Petani itu menanam tanaman padi di sawah)

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan Verb+s (grow+s) karena subject singular (The Farmer)

Structure:

Subject => The farmer V1 (-s/es) => grows Object => The rice plantComplement => in the field

2.have in I some banana trees the garden

=> I have some banana trees in the garden

(Saya mempunyai banya pohon pisang di kebun)

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan Verb 1 (have) karena subject plural (I)

Structure:

Subject => I Verb 1 => HaveObject => Banana TreesComplement => in the garden

3.edelweiss not grow does in Mr.Hadi field his

= Mr. Hadi does not grow edelweiss in his field

(Pak Hadi tidak menanam edelweiss di kebunnya)

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan does karena subject singular (Mr. Hadi)terdapata not yg berarti kalimat bentuk negative

Structure:

Subject: Mr. Hadido/does => does not => not Verb 1 => growObject => edelweissComplement => in his field

4.your does father plant in field his oranges

=> does your father plant orange in his field?

(Apakah ayahmu menanam jeruk di kebunnya?)

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan does karena subject singular (your father)Ada Verb 1 tanpa -s/es (plant) dan tidak ada kata not maka kalimat bentuk interrogative (?)

Structure:

Do/Does => DoesSubject => your fatherVerb 1 => plantObject => orangeComplement => in his field

5.mount lawu in are there many pines

=> There are many pines in Mount Lawu

(Ada banyak pinus di gunung lawu)

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan There is/There are tergantung Object jamak atau tunggal.Many pines (Banyak Pinus), menunjukkan jamak

Structure:

There is => TunggalThere are => JamakObject => many pinesComplement => in Mount Lawu

KESIMPULANBerdasarkan Rumus Present Tense:

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/Complement

1. The farmer grows the rice plant in the field

2. I have some banana trees in the garden

(-) S + does/do + not + V1 + O/Complement

3. Mr. Hadi does not grow eldelweiss in his field

(?) Does/Do + S + V1 + O/Complement?

4. does your father plant orange in his field?

Berdasarkan Rumus There is/There are

(+) there + is/are + O/Complement
