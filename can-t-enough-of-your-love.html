9. How and when to used of enough


I don’t have enough money. The car is very expensive and I will never be rich enough to buy it.

Look at the sentence above, it uses enough in two different ways. Can you see them? What is different about the way we use enough?

There are two examples in the sentence. Let’s look at them separately:

1. "I don’t have enough money."

Money is a noun, so it comes after enough.

2. "I will never be rich enough."

Rich is an adjective so it comes before enough.

Let’s look at the two rules:

1. Enough + noun e.g. "There is not enough food for everybody, go and buy some more."

2. Adjective + enough e.g. "He is not tall enough to play basketball."

Remember! Adjective before, noun after.

12. What is the difference of "too" and "enough"


too
biasanya dipakai sebagai keterangan
seperti "too bad" = terlalu buruk
tapi bisa juga "me too!" = saya juga! (kembali lagi fungsinya sebagai keterangan)

enough

seperti "i have enough courage" = aku punya cukup keberanian (pernyataan)

25. Change the sentence below into positive form using "too" !1. Your motorcycle did not run fast enough on the race. 2. Carmen can lift the chair herself. It's not heavy enough.


Category : Adverb of Degree

Sub-category : Too vs Enough

Level : JHS

Code : 5

Keywords : too, enough, adjective

-------------

Too dan enough digunakan untuk menerangkan adjective, adverb, atau noun.  

Too and enough menyatakan intensitas.


Too berarti "terlalu". Too memiliki arti negatif.

Too digunakan sebelum adjective atau adverb.

Contoh : He is too young to go to school.


Enough berarti "cukup".  

Enough diletakkan setelah adjective atau adverb.

Contoh : He is old enough to go to school.


Enough diletakkan sebelum kata benda.

Contoh : I have enough money.


not enough = kebalikan dari too.

Contoh : not young enough = too old

-----------------

1. Your motorcycle did not run fast enough on the race.

Your motorcycle ran too slowly on the race.


2. Carmen can lift the chair herself. It's not heavy enough.

34. What is the function of "too" and "enough" ?


Kata 'too and enough' dalam bahasa Inggris menyatakan jumlah tertentu, terlalu banyak atau terlalu sedikit.

TOO = lebih dari yang diperlukan

ENOUGH = sesuai jumlah yang diperlukan

NOT ENOUGH = kurang dari yang diperlukan/tidak mencukupi.

Bentuk umum dari penggunaan TOO adalah:

TOO + ADJECTIVE + INFINITY CLAUSE

Alex is too young to drive. (Alex masih terlalu muda untuk menyetir mobil = belum cukup umur)The test is too difficult for me to pass. ( Ujiannya terlalu sulit untuk saya bisa lulus)The tea is too sweet, she put too much sugar in it. (Tehnya terlalu manis, terlalu banyak gula)

Too dalam kalimat diatas menerangkan kata adjective yang mengikutinya, jumlahnya  tidak sesuai (below or above the desire amount)

Bentuk umum untuk penggunaan ENOUGH adalah

ADJECTIVE + ENOUGH + INFINITY CLAUSE

Jill is old enough to drive. (Jill cukup umur untuk menyetir)The test is easy enough for me to pass (Ujiannya cukup mudah untuk saya bisa lulus)The tea is sweet enough (Tehnya cukup manis)

Kata enough dalam kalimat diatas menerangkan bahwa adjective dalam kalimat tersebut jumlahnya sesuai (within what is desirable).
