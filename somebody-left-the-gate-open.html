(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

(?) do/does + S + v1 + o?

Active: How much does water cover the earth's surface?

Present Tense => Passive

(?) is/am/are + S + V3 + by (agent)?

(+) did + S + v1 + o?

Past Tense => Passive

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O

Active: The security locks the school gate at 7.00 every morning

Present Tense => Passive

S + is/am/are + V3 + by (agent)

Passive: the school gate is locked by the security at 07.00 every morning

﻿Height = H

﻿Length = L

no. 2 = gate on the sketch

﻿Length gate on cm = 6 m x 100 cm

﻿= 600 cm

﻿Height gate on cm = 2 m x 100 cm

﻿= 200 cm

Formula;

[tex]\frac{a1}{a2}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{b1}{b2}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{height 1}{height 2} = \frac{length 1}{length 2}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{height1}{200 cm}[/tex]= [tex]\frac{15 cm}{600 cm}[/tex]

18. Change into passive voice 1.He wrote many books 2.She returned all the books 3.Somebody left the gate open 4.The police caught the thieves 5.The woman hit his parked car 6.Pierre and Marie Curie discovered radium and polonium 7.The convenience store sold fresh 8.They interrupted the presentasion 9.did you touch his things? 10.She revealed the secret of her success


Passive voice  

1. Many books were written (by him).

2. All the books were returned (by her).

3. The gate was left open (by somebody).

4. The thieves were caught by the police.

5. His parked car was hit by the woman.

6. Radium and polonium was discovered by Pierre an Marie Curie.

7. Fresh (food) was sold by the convenience store.

8. The presentation was interrupted (by them).

9. Were his things touched (by you)?

10. The secret of her success was revealed (by her).

------------

Explanation

Kalimat pasif adalah kalimat di mana subjeknya dikenai aksi yang didefinisikan oleh kata kerja dalam kalimat tersebut.

Konstruksi umum kalimat pasif adalah :

S + be + past participle  (v₃)

Di mana subjek dari kalimat pasif merupan objek dalam kalimat aktifnya.

Contoh:

Active: John calls Mary.

Passive: Mary is called by John.

Karena semua kalimat aktif dalam soal menggunakan past simple (dapat dilihat dari penggunaan v₂), maka 'be' dalam kalimat pasifnya adalah 'was/were'.

Note: Jika subjek dalam kalimat aktif adalah personal pronoun (I, you, he, etc) atau indefinite pronoun (somebody, someone, etc) maka frasa 'by...' boleh bahkan dianjurkan untuk dihilangkan.
