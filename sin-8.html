1. 8 sin 70°sin 50° sin 10°


2 sin a cos b = sin (a + b) + sin (a - b)
2 cos a sin b = sin (a + b) - sin (a - b)
2 cos a cos b = cos (a + b) + cos (a - b)
-2 sin a sin b = cos (a + b) - cos (a - b)

Jadi nilai dari :

8 sin 70° sin 50° sin 10°

= -4 . -2 sin 70° sin 50° sin 10°

= -4 [cos (70° + 50°) - cos (70° - 50°)] sin 10°

= -4 (cos 120° - cos 20°) . sin 10°

= -4 (-1/2 - cos 20°) . sin 10°

= (2 + 4 cos 20°) . sin 10°

= 2 sin 10° + 4 cos 20° . sin 10°

= 2 sin 10° + 2 . 2 cos 20° sin 10°

= 2 sin 10° + 2 [sin (20° + 10°) - sin (20° - 10°)]

= 2 sin 10° + 2 (sin 30° - sin 10°)

= 2 sin 10° + 2 (1/2 - sin 10°)

= 2 sin 10° + 1 - 2 sin 10°

= 1

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin(90^\circ-3x)&=\sin 2x\\\cos 3x&=2\sin x\cos x\\4\cos^3 x-3\cos x&=2\sin x\cos x\\4\cos^2 x-3&=2\sin x\\4(1-\sin^2 x)-3-2\sin x&=0\\-4\sin^2 x-2\sin x+1&=0\\4\sin^2 x+2\sin x-1&=0\\\sin x&=\frac{-2\pm\sqrt{2^2-4(4)(-1)}}{2(4)}\\\sin x&=\frac{-1\pm\sqrt{5}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Karena sin 18° di kuadran I maka bernilai + sehingga

[tex]\displaystyle \sin 18^\circ=\frac{-1+\sqrt{5}}{4}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned}\cos 36^\circ&=1-2\sin^2 18^\circ\\&=1-2\left ( \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4} \right )^2\\&=1-\frac{3-\sqrt{5}}{4}\\&=\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

sin 54° = sin (90° - 36°) = cos 36°

sehingga

[tex]\begin{aligned}8\sin 18^\circ\sin 54^\circ&=8\left ( \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4} \right )\left ( \frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{4} \right )\\&=\frac{5-1}{2}\\&=2\end{aligned}[/tex]

17. Nilai dari 8 sin 18.sin 36 adalah


Jawab:

[tex]\displaystyle 2\sqrt{5-2\sqrt{5}}[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Tentukan sin 18°

Misal A = 18° → 5A = 90°

3A + 2A = 90°

sin 2A = sin (90° - 3A)

2 sin A cos A = cos 3A

2 sin A cos A = 4 cos³ A - 3 cos A

2 sin A = 4 cos² A - 3

4 cos² A - 2 sin A - 3 = 0

4(1 - sin² A) - 2 sin A - 3 = 0

-4 sin² A - 2 sin A + 1 = 0

4 sin² A + 2 sin A - 1 = 0

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin A&\:=\frac{-2\pm\sqrt{2^2-4(4)(-1)}}{2(4)}\\\:&=\frac{-1\pm\sqrt{5}}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Karena sin 18° di kuadran I maka bernilai positif sehingga

[tex]\displaystyle \sin 18^\circ=\frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4}[/tex]

Untuk menentukan sin 36° tentukan dulu cos 36° dari rumus cos 2A = 1 - 2 sin² A

[tex]\begin{aligned}\cos 36^\circ&\:=1-2\sin^2 18^\circ\\\:&=1-2\left ( \frac{6-2\sqrt{5}}{16} \right )\\\:&=\frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{4}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Tentukan sin 36° dari rumus sin² x = 1 - cos² x

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sin 36^\circ&\:=\sqrt{1-\cos^2 36^\circ}\\\:&=\sqrt{1-\frac{6+2\sqrt{5}}{16}}\\\:&=\sqrt{\frac{5-\sqrt{5}}{8}}\\\:&=\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{5-\sqrt{5}}{2}}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Selesaikan soal

[tex]\begin{aligned}8\sin 18^\circ\sin 36^\circ&\:=8~\frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4}~\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{5-\sqrt{5}}{2}}\\\:&=\sqrt{\left ( \sqrt{5}-1 \right )^2}\sqrt{\frac{5-\sqrt{5}}{2}}\\\:&=\sqrt{\frac{\left ( 6-2\sqrt{5} \right )\left ( 5-\sqrt{5} \right )}{2}}\\\:&=\sqrt{20-8\sqrt{5}}\\\:&=\sqrt{4(5-2\sqrt{5})}\\\:&=2\sqrt{5-2\sqrt{5}}\end{aligned}[/tex]
