1. An eagle is 4.56 m above the sea level while a seahorse is 6.82 m below the sea level. Adrian is in the middle between the eagle and the seahorse a)Calculate the vertical distance between the eagle and the seahorse.​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Jarak elang dari permukaan laut = 4,56 m

Jarak kuda laut dari permukaan laut = -6,82 m

Adrian berada di tengah-tengah mereka.

Jarak antara elang dan kuda laut yaitu

= 4,56 - (-6,82)

= 11,38 m

Posisi Adrian dari permukaan laut

= (4,56 + (-6,82)) ÷ 2

= -2,26 ÷ 2

= -1,13

Posisi Adrian dari permukaan laut adalah 1,13 m di bawah permukaan laut

Sea horses are marine animals known for their unique body shape resembling a horse, with legs that they use to cling onto the ocean floor or plants. They have a large head and a long neck. There are around 54 species known in the world, with sizes varying from around 2.5 cm to 35 cm. Sea horses are also known for their unique reproductive behavior, where males carry fertilized eggs and then give birth to the offspring.

38. apa itu "vertebrate" dan "invertebrate?"


Vertebrates are animals that have a backbone inside their body. The major groups include fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds and mammals. Invertebrates don't have a backbone. They either have a soft body, like worms and jellyfish, or a hard outer casing covering their body, like spiders and crabs.
