6. 15. Sekar (run) to the park last week.a. runsC. runningb. rund. ran​


Sekar ran to the park last week.

Jawabannya adalah D. Ran

PEMBAHASAN Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan di masa lalu atau Peristiwa masa lampau. Waktunya:

=> yesterday

=> Last night

=> Last week ✔️

=> two days ago

=> two weeks ago

Rumus Past Tense

(+) S + Verb-2

(-) S + did not + Verb-1

(?) Did + S + Verb-1?

Berdasarkan pilihan ganda:

a. runs

=> Verb+s => utk Subjek tunggal Present Tense

b. run

=> Verb-1=> utk Subjek jamak Present Tense

c. running

=> Verb-ing => utk Continuous Tense

d. ran ✔️

=> Verb-2 => utk Past Tense

KESIMPULAN

Sekar ran to the park last week.

Keterangan:Dalam soal ada keterangan Last week yg berarti lampau Maka menggunakan Past Tense yaitu Verb-2 ran

7. On my way to the campus, I ... a friend whom I had not met for me a. ran into b. ran over c. ran down d. ran out


On my way to the campus, I ran into a friend whom I had not met for me.

Bila diartikan: Dalam perjalanan ke kampus, saya bertemu seorang teman yang saya tidak bertemu.

Pembahasan

The meaning of the phrasal verb:

A. Ran into = cost (harga), meet by accident (bertemu dengan tidak sengaja)

B. Ran over = explain quickly (menerangkan dengan cepat), hit with a vehicle (tertabrak kendaraan)

C. Ran down =

Hit a pedestrian with a vehicle (menabrak seorang pejalan kaki dengan kendaraan)Lose energy or power (kehabisan tenaga)Criticise, disparage (mengkritik)Find the source or origin of something (mencari sumber atau asal sesuatu)

D. Ran out = Have none left (habis)
