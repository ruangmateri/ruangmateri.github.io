5. Do you think the prince will stay a fish forever?


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: narrative
Kata kunci: prince, fish, forever
Pembahasan:

Well, the answer to this question can be various. In my opinion, the prince will possibly stay a fish if he can't find any magical stuff to turn him  back to his own nature. Yet, if he can, he may have a chance to do so. For example, if he suddenly finds a magic diamond in the ocean which belong to God of Sea, Neptune, he may be back to human form. Or if he is suddenly caught by the loveliest princess, and she fancies the fish prince plus keep it, then he may. Or, if he has eaten the flesh of a dead body in the sea or maybe bit the fresh human flesh and drunk the blood of human, he can change back.
So, it is just a matter of creativity of how you think the story ends. It can be made into any tone, serious, witty, horror etc.
