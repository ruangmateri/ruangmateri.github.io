20. 10 contoh kalimat opinion dalam bentuk bahasa inggris


Kalimat opinion dalam bahasa inggris merupakan jenis kalimat yang digunaka untuk mengungkapkan pendapat. Kalimat opinion dapat dinyatakan dengan penggunaan expression of giving opinion (ungkapan pemberian pendapat). Berikut penjelasannya.

Penjelasan:

Expression of giving opinion merupakan ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyampaikan pendapat, ide, gagasan, maupun pandangan terhadap sesuatu. Berikut beberapa expression of giving opinion yang umum digunakan:

1. I think (that) ...

2. I feel (that) ...

3. In my opinion, ...

4. For me, ...

5. It is my opinion that ...

Berikut contoh kalimat yang menggunakan expression of giving opinion.

1. I think we should bring an umbrella.

2. In my opinion, this restaurant is expensive.

3. I feel Captain America is better than Ironman.

4. I think I have to tell you the truth.

5. It is my opinion that we have to rearrange the structure of the organization.

6. For me, it is better to discuss it later.

7. I think this cake is delicious.

8. I feel that global economic collapse is caused by corona virus.

9. It is my opinion that we should provide health insurance for the staffs.

10. For me, money is not everything but everything needs money.
