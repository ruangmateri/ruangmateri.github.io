Jawaban:

grammar

1.tense

Tense yang harus dipakai ketika kita menceritakan daily routine adalah present simple tense. Present simple adalah tense yang paling mudah dan mendasar. Berikut adalah strukturnya:

Subject + verb

Present simple menggunakan verb satu atau v1. Oleh karena itu, v1 harus ditambahkan “s” diakhirnya jika subjeknya adalah orang ketiga tunggal (he, she, it). Contoh:

I eat

You eat

We eat

They eat

She eats

He eats

It eats

2.Adverb of time

Kita akan membutuhkan adverb of time atau kata keterangan waktu ketika kita mendeskripsikan daily routine. Berikut adalah beberapa adverb of time yang bisa digunakan:

Always (selalu)

Never (tidak pernah)

Sometimes (kadang-kadang)

Usually (biasanya)

Often (sering)

Frequently (sering)

Before (sebelum)

After (sesudah)

Then (kemudian)

Later (kemudian)

First (pertama)

Next (selanjutnya)

Generally (biasanya)

Contoh:

I always wake up at 5 in the morning. (Aku selalu bangun pukul 5 di pagi hari)

I drink coffee before I eat breakfast. (Aku minum kopi sebelum sarapan)

Next, I watch TV then do homework. (Selanjutnya, aku menonton TV kemudian mengerjakan PR)

natasiarumondang

To talk about daily activities, we need to use simple present tense. Let’s discuss about simple present tense briefly before we move on to the answer part.

Pembahasan

Simple present is used for actions that happen over and over (repeated actions), true facts, and schedules. Basic pattern of simple present is subject + base verb/ base verb -s/-es.

To make questions: Do/Does + subject + base verb

1. Repeated actions

To talk about repeated actions, we need to use time markers (words about time) that can help to show how often an action happens. Common time markers are: every, once, twice, three times, always, often, usually, sometimes, never. This pattern is good to talk about daily activities.

Note: Always (100%), often & usually (80%), sometimes (50%), rarely (5%-10%), never (0%).

Examples:

- I brush my teeth every day.

- My sister writes diary every night.

- My mother cooks twice a day.

- I usually get up early.

- Doni never arrives late at school.

- Do you do your homework every day?

2. True facts

We can also use simple present to talk about facts or true informations.

Examples:

- Water freezes at 0°C

- Indonesia people celebrates Independence Day on August the 17th.

3. Schedule

Use simple present when we write or talk about our schedule.

Examples:

- My favorite TV show starts at 8:00 pm.

- Our school bus leave the shelter at 6:30 AM and 5:30 PM.

- What time does your school start?

Answers

10 examples of simple present questions for daily activity exercise.

1. What do you do every morning?

Sample answer: I eat breakfast every morning.

2. Do you wash your face every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I do.

3. How often do you play sports?

Sample answer: I play badminton twice a week.

4. Do drink water every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I drink water in the morning and after lunch.

5. What do you eat for breakfast?

Sample answer: I usually eat toast for breakfast.

6. What time is your English class?

Sample answer: My English class is at 1:00 pm on Wednesday and Friday.

7. What do you do after school?

Sample answer: I usually do my homework.

8. Where do you eat lunch at your break time?

Sample answer: I always eat at the school cafeteria.

9. Do you help your mother every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I do. I help my mother to wash dishes after dinner.

10. What do you do before school?

Sample answer: I usually prepare my uniform and check my homework.

23. Contoh Dialog percakapan Daily Routine 2 orang, Tolonggg ini Tugass


Jawaban:

Berikut contoh percakapan rutin sehari-hari antara dua orang:

Orang 1: Selamat pagi! Bagaimana tidurmu tadi malam?

Orang 2: Selamat pagi! Saya tidur cukup nyenyak, terima kasih sudah bertanya. Bagaimana denganmu?

Orang 1: Saya juga tidur nyenyak. Apa yang biasanya kamu lakukan di pagi hari?

Orang 2: Saya biasanya bangun jam 6 pagi, mandi, dan sarapan. Lalu saya pergi bekerja.

Orang 1: Kedengarannya seperti rutinitas yang bagus. Apa yang kamu lakukan setelah bekerja?

Orang 2: Sepulang kerja, saya biasanya pergi ke gym sekitar satu jam, lalu pulang dan makan malam. Setelah itu, saya menonton TV atau membaca buku sebelum tidur.

Orang 1: Itu cara yang bagus untuk melepas lelah setelah hari yang melelahkan. Saya biasanya hanya pulang dan menonton TV. Mungkin aku harus mulai pergi ke gym juga.

Orang 2: Ya, ini cara yang bagus untuk tetap sehat dan menghilangkan stres. Anda harus mencobanya!

Penjelasan:

English

Person 1: Good morning! How did you sleep last night?

Person 2: Good morning! I slept pretty well, thanks for asking. How about you?

Person 1: I slept well too. What do you usually do in the morning?

Person 2: I usually wake up at 6 am, take a shower, and have breakfast. Then I go to work.

Person 1: That sounds like a good routine. What do you do after work?

Person 2: After work, I usually go to the gym for an hour or so, then I go home and have dinner. After that, I watch some TV or read a book before going to bed.

Person 1: That's a nice way to unwind after a long day. I usually just go home and watch TV. Maybe I should start going to the gym too.

Person 2: Yeah, it's a great way to stay healthy and relieve stress. You should give it a try!
