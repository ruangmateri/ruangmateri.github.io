2. 10 sentences that contain appositive phrase​


Jawaban:

1. My sister Jane is 27 years old. (Jane renames sister)

2. My mom, who is a nurse, drives a red car. (Who is a nurse renames mom, but it is not essential for the meaning of the sentence.)

3. The boy who painted this picture is named Kevin. (Who painted this picture renames boy, and it is essential for the meaning of the sentence.)

4. Sarah's dog Rover is a golden retriever. (Rover renames dog.)

5. That ladybug, an insect, just landed on the rose bush.

6. Mr. Harrison, the principal at my school, wears a tie every day.

7. I like spaghetti, an Italian dish with noodles and sauce.

8. Marcus, my sister's hamster, likes to run in a wheel.

9. The play was brilliant, and I enjoyed Mary, the best actress in our school.

10. I really like my grand father's horse Chester.

Penjelasan:

Appositives are nouns, noun phrases, or noun clauses that rename a noun that comes just before them. Remember that an appositive can be a single word or several words.

Underline each noun clause in the sentences below. In the space on the left, indicate whether the noun clause is used as a subject (s), object (0), complement (C), or appositive (AP).

Example Ed saw that many people had already left.

1. Whoever reads the novels of Faulkner and Hemingway will have a better

understanding of people. (Noun clause as subject)

Siapapun yang membaca novel karya Faulkner and Hemingway akan memiliki pemahaman yang lebih baik mengenai orang-orang

2. When he was trying to break into the movies, Marion Morrison decided that he would change his name to John Wayne. (Noun clause as complement)

Ketika dia mencoba memasuki bioskop, Marion Morrison memutuskan bahwa dia akan mengubah namanya menjadi John Wayne

3. We will sell our house to whomever you suggest. (Noun clause as object)

Kami akan menjual rumah kami kepda siapapun yang kamu sarankan

4. Their complaint that there was no heat was ignored. (Noun clause as complement)

Komplain mereka bahwa tidak ada pemanas diabaikan

5. At the end of the evening, he was asked when they might go out again (Noun clause as complement)

Di akhir malam, dia diminta kapan mereka mungkin pergi lagi

6. When he released the ball, he knew that he had thrown a strike (Noun clause as complement)

Ketika dia melemparkan bola, dia tahu bahwa dia melempar tendangan

7. In writing your term paper, you may draw material from whatever has been written on the subject. (Noun clause as object)

Dalam menulis makalah, kamu mungkin menulis bahan-bahan dari apapun telah ditulis pada subyeknya

8. When we leave is important. (Noun clause as subject)

Ketika yang kami tinggalkan adalah penting

9. His problem is that he is lazy. (Noun clause as complement)

Masalahnya adalah bahwa dia malas

10. The ancient Egyptians believed that the pharaoh was immort​al Noun (clause as complement)

Mesir kuno percaya bahwa Firaun adalah abadi

Pembahasan

Noun clause adalah sekelompok kata berfungsi sebagai noun. Noun clause dapat dgunakan sebagai subyek, obyek, atau pelengkap.

1. Sebagai subyek

Whatever makes you cry is hurting me

Whoever comes late should buy the snacks for us

2. Sebagai obyek

My cats eat whatever food I give her

You may go wherever you want to

3. Sebagai complement

My dream is that my parents can reunite again

I can't even think that she leaves me alone here

Clause merupakan sekelompok kata yang terdiri dari subjek dan kata kerja. Dalam kalimat diatas kita dapat melihat clausenya yaitu:

1.  Object clouse: Opportunities which can develop their personalities

2. subject clouse: That frustration and anxiety may be the cause of some violent acts...

3. subject clouse: That he is insane...

4. object clouse: where he had put the knife.

5. object clouse: whoever could answer the questions.

6. subject clouse: The problems, wheather or not he could win the case,

Pembahasan

Clause terbagi ke dalam dua bentuk yaitu independent clause dan dependent clause:

- Independent clause adalah clause yang bisa berdiri sendiri serta dianggap sebuah kalimat utuh.

- Dependent clause membutuhkan clause lain untuk membentuk kalimat utuh. Contoh:

1. He order something when he need.

He order something = independent clause

when he need = dependent clause

2. She has talk to her mother who she missed so much.

She has talk to her mother = independent clause

who she missed so much = dependent clause

Kata ini seringkali didampingi oleh modifier (kata yang menerangkan appositive) membentuk appositive phrase. Dengan demikian, frasa ini umumnya berupa noun phrase.
