1) I tried that, but…

2) That’s a good idea, but …

3) No, I’d rather not.

4) Sorry, I can't.


4. berikan masing - masing 5 contoh kalimat asking , giving and offer recommendation ?


Recommendation is a suggestion or piece of advice about how to solve a problem, deal with a situation, etc.

Sedangkan suggestion is an idea or plan that you offer for someone to consider.

Jadi, terdapat perbedaan antara recommendation dan suggestion.

Contoh kalimat asking for recommendation (menanyakan rekomendasi):

1. Could you put in a good word for me?

2. Could you let others know about this experience?

3. Could you pass this on to others?

4. Would you mind sharing your experience?

5. Would you add me to your contact list?

Contoh kalimat giving recommendation (memberikan rekomendasi):

1. I highly recommend hiring this person.

2. This person is very professional.

3. We found the information presented very useful.

4. I only have positive things to say about this this person.

5. Please mention my name when you contact them.

Contoh kalimat offer recommendation (menawarkan rekomendasi):

1. I would be happy to give you their contact information.

2. I am happy to recommend this person to help you.

3. I am pleased to provide a reference for that position.

4. I can offer only the highest recommendation for your office.

5. i am honored to support him as a candidate.

Berikut adalah contoh teks ekspositori dan identifikasi thesis, argument, dan rekomendasi:

Judul: Pengaruh Penggunaan Internet pada Prestasi Belajar Siswa

Thesis: Penggunaan internet memiliki pengaruh yang besar pada prestasi belajar siswa, baik positif maupun negatif.

Argument:

Positif: internet mempermudah akses terhadap sumber belajar dan informasi yang berkualitas, sehingga membantu siswa dalam meningkatkan prestasi belajar.Negatif: penggunaan internet yang tidak terkontrol dapat menimbulkan dampak negatif seperti membuat siswa terjebak dalam hal-hal yang tidak bermanfaat, seperti browsing dan bermain game.

Rekomendasi:

Siswa harus bijak dan cerdas dalam menggunakan internet, memprioritaskan hal-hal yang bermanfaat dan menghindari hal-hal yang merugikan.Orang tua dan guru harus memberikan pendidikan dan bimbingan bagi siswa agar dapat menggunakan internet dengan baik dan bijak.Pemerintah harus memperkuat regulasi dan pengawasan terhadap penggunaan internet oleh pelajar agar tidak terjadi hal-hal yang merugikan.

Demikianlah contoh teks ekspositori yang mengidentifikasi thesis, argument, dan rekomendasi.

31. contoh Giving a recommendation, responding a recommendation, expressing an offer, responding an offer


kelas : XI
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Expressions
Kata kunci : giving recommendation, responding a recommendation, giving an offer, responding an offer

pembahasan:
Giving recommendation adalah ungkapan yang menyatakan saran. Sedangkan giving an offer adalah ungkapan yang menyarankan sesuatu.

Rumus giving recommendation expressions pada suasana yang tidak mendesak
- subject + should/ought to + verb1
1) I suggest that you ...
2) I recommend that you ...
3) You really should/ought to ...
4) (I’d say that) You’d better ...
5) If I were you, I would ...
6) It would probably a good idea to ...
7) Why don’t you ...?
8) How about ...?
9) Maybe you should ...
10) I think you should/ought to ...

Contoh giving recommendation:
1) (Formal) : Your shoes looks so dirty. You should clean it.
2) (Formal) : I suggest that we do this project together.
3) (Formal) : I think you should go and help your mother.
4) (Informal) : What about going to the restaurant now?.
5) (Informal) : How about taking the bus instead of driving?

Accepting a recommendation:
1) Yes, I’d like/ love to.
2) That sounds like a good idea.
3) Thank you.
4) Why didn’t I think of that?

Refusing a recommendation:
1) I tried that, but…
2) That’s a good idea, but …
3) No, I’d rather not.
4) Sorry, I can't.

Rumus giving a recommendation dalam situasi yang mendesak:
- Subject + had better + verb1
Contoh :
You look so pale. You had better go to the doctor.

Giving an offer expressions:
- Can I ...?
- May I ...?
- Shall I ...?


Contoh giving an offer:
1) Can I help you?
2) May I read this book for him?
3) Shall I carry your bag to your room?
4) Shall I get you a bottle of water?

Accepting an offer:
1) Yes, please!
2) That would be very nice.
3) Thank you.
4) What kind of you.

Refusing an offer:
1) Thank you, but I can't do it myself.
2) No, thank you.

35. Contoh hortatory exposition beserta general structurenya (thesis, arguments, recommendation)!


Thesis:
  Except foe weekends, people often ignore their breakfast. People are so busy that they often skip breakfast. Meanwhile, breakfast is the most important meal of the day.
Argument:
   Eating breakfast may reduce your hunger later in the day, which may make it easier to avoid overeating. When you skip breakfast, you may feel ravenous later and be tempted to reach for a quick fix - such as vending machine candy or doughnuts at the office. In addition, the prolonged fasting that occurs when you skip breakfast can increase your body's insulin response, which in turn increases fat storage and weight gain. In fact, skipping breakfast actually increases your risk of obesity.
    Eating breakfast may give you energy, increasing your physical activity during the day. A healthy breakfast refuels your body and replenishes the glycogen stores that supply your muscles with immediate energy. Skipping breakfast is associated with decreased physical activity.
Recommendation:
    Here are some tips to help you get the most out of breakfast time. Eat your breakfast early. Aim to enjoy breakfast shortly after you wake up. If you're not into eating early, have your first meal within an hour each morning to sit down and eat. This way you can step back, savor your meal, and focus on your upcoming.
