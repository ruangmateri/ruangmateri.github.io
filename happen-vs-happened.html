5. 50. If the police didn't come on time, riot would happen.The fact is_________A. If the police didn't come on time, so riot wouldhappenedB. The police came on time, but riot wouldhappenedC. The police don't come on time, so riot willhappenedD. The police came on time, so riot will not happenE. The police come on time and riot will nothappen​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCES

If the police didn't come on time, riot would happen.

The fact is :

E. The polices come on time and riot will not happen

EXPLANATION

A Conditional sentence is divided into four types :

⏺ ZERO CONDITIONAL

The Zero Conditional is used to describe the phenomenon, habit, and General truth.

Pattern :

If+S+verb¹ (s/es)+O , S+verb¹ (s/es)+O

Example. :

If we put an empty aerosol bottle on bonfire, it explodes.

Fact : An empty aerosol bottle explodes when we heat it on bonfire because it contains propellants.

⏺ FIRST CONDITIONAL

The First Conditional is used to describe the truth in the present/future. The condition will probably happen.

Pattern :

If +S+ verb¹ (s/es) + O, S+will+verb¹+O

Example :

If the police don't come on time, riot will happen

Fact : Riot will probably happen.

⏺ SECOND CONDITIONAL

The second Conditional is used to describe the untruth in the present / future. The speaker has an intention but he/she can't afford his/her dream comes true in the present/future (improbable).

Pattern:

If+S+verb²+O/C , S+would+verb¹+O/C

Example :

If the polices didn't come on time, riot would happen.

Fact :  ➡ The polices come on time, so riot will not happen➡ Riot won't happen and the polices come on time

⏺ THIRD CONDITIONAL

The Third Conditional is used to describe the untruth in the past. It reflects a regret for something that the speaker couldn't afford to do in the past.

Pattern :

If+S+Had+Verb³+O , S+Would+have+Verb³+O

Example :

If the polices had not come on time, riot would have happened.Fact :  ➡ The polices came on time, so riot didn't happen.➡ Riot didn't happen and the polices came on time

Passive Voice

Passive voice atau yang dalam bahasa Indonesia disebut kalimat pasif adalah kalimat yang mengubah kata kerja dari me- menjadi di- dan juga merubah object menjadi subject.

Rumus umum passive voice adalah:

TO BE + V3

Jika dijabarkan, maka akan menjadi sebagai berikut:

Simple Present = is/am/are + V3Present Continous = is/am/are + being +V3Simple Past = was/ were + V3Past Continous = was/ were being + V3Simple Future = will be +V3Future continous = will be being + V3Present Perfect = have/has been + V3Past perfect = Had been + V3

22. beda nya happend, happen, happened, sama happens apa sih? pusing nih bantu ya..


Kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : verb
Kata kunci : happen, present verb, past verb, past participle verb

Pembahasan:
Happen (verb), yang membedakannya:
1. Happen dan happens digunakan untuk present tense dan future tense. Yang membedakan adalah subject-nya.
I/you/they/we + happen
She/he/it/name + happens
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + will + happen
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + am/is/are+was/were + going to + happen
Contoh:
It happens regularly.
Flood will happen if we throw rubbish everywhere.

2. Happened, digunakan untuk past tense dan past participle tense.
Contoh:
(past tense - v2) It happened last year.
(past participle - v3) That thing would have happened if I hadnot studied hard.
