୭ What Is Prefer? ୭

Patterns:

Subject + prefer/prefers + V-ing(1) + to + V-ing(2)

Subject + prefer/prefers + Noun(1) + to + Noun(2)

Subject + prefer/prefers + Adjective(1) (NOUN) + to + Adjective(2) (NOUN).

୭ Example ୭

1. Prefer + noun + to + noun

୭ Example ୭

‧ He prefers orange to banana.

‧ The boys prefer watch cartoon to watch action film.

‧ I prefer gado-gado to steak.

2. prefer + v-ing + to + v-ing

୭ Example ୭

‧ Yuri prefers going out to fishing.

‧ Taeyeon and Tiffany prefer playing badminton to playing tennis.

Note: Di dalam kalimat positif, apabila subject dari kalimat itu adalah she/he/it maka kata prefer di tambah ‘s’.

3. Prefer + to infinitive

୭  Example ୭

‧ She prefers to make a cake.

‧ The man prefers to practice.

12. Task 14 Rewrite the sentences using would rather ... than. Examples: He prefers milk to coffee. He would rather have milk than coffee. She likes studying better than listening to music. She would rather study than listen to music. 1. They prefer staying at home to going out. 2. My uncle likes travelling by ship better than by plane. 3. I prefer young people to old people. 4. She likes washing dishes better than cooking. 5. We like antique cars better than contemporary ones. 6. He prefers drama to novel. 7. Timothy likes natural materials better than synthetic ones. 8. The Browns prefer meat to vegetable. 9. I prefer classical music to jazz music. 10. We like renting a car better than going by bus.​


Jawaban:

【Tips】

Untuk mengubah kalimat dengan menggunakan "would rather ... than", kita perlu menggantikan frasa seperti "prefer", "likes ... better than", dengan "would rather ... than". Ini adalah suatu cara untuk mengekspresikan preferensi seseorang.

【Deskripsi】

Mari kita lakukan pemecahan masalah langkah demi langkah berdasarkan contoh dan pertanyaan yang Anda berikan:

**Pertanyaan**:

1. They prefer staying at home to going out.

**Langkah Pemecahan**:

1. Identifikasi frasa yang menunjukkan preferensi, yaitu "prefer ... to".

2. Gantikan "prefer" dengan "would rather".

3. Gantikan "to" dengan "than".

**Hasil**:

They would rather stay at home than go out.

Dengan mengikuti langkah-langkah ini untuk setiap kalimat, kita akan mendapatkan:

1. They would rather stay at home than go out.

2. My uncle would rather travel by ship than by plane.

3. I would rather be with young people than old people.

4. She would rather wash dishes than cook.

5. We would rather have antique cars than contemporary ones.

6. He would rather watch a drama than read a novel.

7. Timothy would rather use natural materials than synthetic ones.

8. The Browns would rather eat meat than vegetables.

9. I would rather listen to classical music than jazz music.

10. We would rather rent a car than go by bus.

**Kesimpulan**:

Pada saat melakukan perubahan dari "prefer" atau "likes ... better than" ke "would rather ... than", pastikan untuk juga mengubah bentuk kata kerja agar sesuai dengan struktur "would rather".

【Hasil】

Kita telah berhasil mengubah semua kalimat dengan menggunakan struktur "would rather ... than" untuk mengekspresikan preferensi.
