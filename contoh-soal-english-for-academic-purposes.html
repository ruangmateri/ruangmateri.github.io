9. What are the three general purposes for giving speeches?


Jawaban:

The general purpose of a speech is usually formulated in three ways:

1. To inform (informative)

Contains information, reminds about something and some important things.

2. To influence (persuasive)

Invite the public, bring and influence to do something according to the wishes of the contents of the speech.

3. To entertain (recreative).

Aside from being a place of information and influence, speeches are also a place of entertainment for listeners who need enlightenment.

24. purposes of orientation, complication, sequence of events, resolution, and coda in English


Generic Structure of Narrative Text:
1. Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.(berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat dan waktu terjadinya cerita (siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana)

2. Complication : Where the problems in the story developed. (Permasalahan muncul / mulai terjadi dan berkembang)

3. Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved. Masalah selesai, secara baik “happy ending” ataupun buruk “bad ending”.

4. Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story

33. Apa itu academic reading


Academic reading diartikan sebagai kegiatan membaca dengan materi-materi yang terfokus pada bidang akademis dan pendidikan. Acadmeic rading berbeda dengan kegiatan membaca lainnya, bukan hanya dari konten bacaan, tapi juga teknik membaca, dan apa yang diharapkan penulis untuk para pembacanya. Contoh academic reading meliputi buku-buku tradisional, kamus, ensiklopedia dan artikel jurnal.

Pembahasan

Berikut ini adalah subkategori pada bagian-bagian academic reading yang bisa kalian pelajari:

1. Subkategori: Critical Reading

Konten: Deep reading, Dialogic reading, Reading and critical thinking

2. Subkategori: Effective Reading

Konten: Identifying text types, Introduction to reading skills, Prediction strategies for reading, Introduction to speed reading, Introduction to scanning, Scanning for specific information, Reading to identify main points, Skim-reading practice

3. Subkategori: Effecrive Note-taking

Konten: The Cornell Method, Organizing notes

4. Subkategori: Academic Journal Articles

Konten: How journals are structured, Accessing journal articles

5. Subkategori: Discourse Community

Konten: Understanding technical language

39. contoh kalimat expressing purposes​


Jawaban:

Berikut 2 contoh kalimat expressing of purpose dari masing-masing pola kalimat

a. So that/in order that + subject +modal

-

I am saving money so that i can buy a new mobile phone

-

He takes english course in order that he can speak english

b. In order (not) to + Infinitive

-

She comes closer to the MC in order to hear the announcement

-

Andy left home early in order not to be late

c. So as (not) to + infinitive

-

He invites my sister to the restaurant so as to have lunch together

- She hides her face so as not to be recognized by her ex-boyfriend
