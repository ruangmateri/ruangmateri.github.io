26. Do you have experience in studying english with mam ira?


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

Do you have any experience in studying English with Ma'am Ira?

No, I don't have any experience in studying English with Ma'am Ira.

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat pertanyaan dari Simple Present Tense karena menanyakan fakta dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Tidak, aku tidak punya pengalaman belajar bahasa Inggris dengan Bu Ira.

Simple Present Tense:

29. My experience during pandemic Covid 19.​


Jawaban:

My experience during pandemic Covid 19.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, my school learning activities that were originally face-to-face have now been replaced by the during method, which is the learning method on / with the internet network via cellphone / smartphone communication tools. During this online learning, I had many experiences, ranging from difficulties, conveniences, and other fun. I felt that learning during this time was quite tiring, because I had a lot more to do, starting from looking for learning support via the internet, sending assignments, making practice / rote videos, and so on. However, they were all quite exciting for me too, and I was quite happy to do all the work. The happiest thing is if my assignment gets good and satisfying grades. I often experience problems in learning during this time, such as internet network disruptions. Also, I experienced a lot of conveniences so that the tasks in my learning during my learning were more easily resolved.

Terjemahan:

Pengalaman saya selama pandemi Covid 19.

Masa pandemi covid - 19, kegiatan belajar sekolah ku yang semula bisa bertatapan muka, kini terganti dengan metode during, yaitu metode pembelajaran dalam / dengan jaringan internet melalui alat komunikasi handphone / smartphone. Selama pembelajaran daring ini, banyak pengalaman yang kualami, mulai dari kesulitan, kemudahan, dan keseruan lainnya. Aku merasa bahwa pembelajaran during ini cukup melelahkan, karena lebih banyak yang harus kukerjakan, mulai dari mencari pendukung pelajaran lewat internet, pengiriman tugas, membuat video praktek / hafalan, dan lainnya. Namun, semua itu cukup menyerukan juga bagiku, dan aku cukup senang melakukan semua tugas itu. Yang paling bahagia adalah jika tugasku mendapatkan nilai yang baik dan memuaskan. Kendala dalam pembelajaran during ini sering kualami, seperti gangguan jaringan internet. Juga, banyak kemudahan yang kualami sehingga tugas - tugas dalam pembelajaran during ku lebih terselesaikan dengan mudah.
