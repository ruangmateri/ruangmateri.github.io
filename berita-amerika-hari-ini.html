Komunikasi menurut Roger (1996) sebagai berikut. “Communication is a process in which participants create and share information with one another in order to reach a mutual understanding.” Ini berarti komunikasi merupakan suatu proses di mana komunikator dan komunikan saling bertukar informasi agar terjadi saling pengertian. Sementara itu yang dimaksud dengan "inovasi" menurut Roger (1996) adalah: “An idea, practice, or object that is perceived as new by individual or other unit of adoption.” Inovasi merupakan gagasan, tindakan atau objek yang dianggap baru oleh seseorang.

Pembahasan

Dalam blognya dengan judul ulasan Kontribusi Teori-teori Komunikasi dalam Komunikasi Inovasi, M Badri menjelaskan, tujuan komunikasi adalah tercapainya suatu pemahaman bersama (mutual understanding) antara dua atau lebih partisipan komunikasi terhadap suatu pesan (dalam hal ini adalah ide baru) melalui saluran komunikasi tertentu. Dalam proses difusi inovasi, komunikasi memiliki peranan penting menuju perubahan sosial sesuai dengan yang dikehendaki. Rogers dan Floyed Shoemaker (1987) menegaskan bahwa “difusi merupakan tipe komunikasi khusus, yaitu mengkomunikasikan inovasi. Ini berarti kajian difusi merupakan bagian kajian komunikasi yang berkaitan dengan gagasan-gagasan baru, sedangkan pengkajian komunikasi meliputi semua bentuk pesan”. Jadi jika yang dikomunikasikan bukan produk inovasi, maka kurang lazim disebut sebagai difusi.

Dalam difusi inovasi saluran komunikasi memiliki karakter kelebihan dan kelemahan masing-masing. Oleh karena itu dalam menggunakan saluran komunikasi ini perlu mempertimbangkan berbagai hal.

Orientation stage  Starting from the outbreak of the "Greater East Asian War", and the United States declared war on Japan because of the attack by Japanese troops at the United States Defense Center "Pearl Harbor" on December 8, 1941. Japanese troops with the Navy and Air Force were increasingly acting aggressively to land in Indochina territory. , Philippines, Malaya and Indonesia.

The Dutch East Indies government joined the Allies in declaring war with Japan. Japan landed in Indonesia with the aim of paralyzing Dutch troops. The Japanese army's first landing on Tarakan then penetrated the Balikpapan, Manado, Ambon, Makassar, Pontianak and Palembang areas. He also controlled the regions in Java. On March 1, 1942, the Japanese landed in Banten, Indramayu and Rembang.

Its territory expanded further with the control of Batavia on March 5, 1942, and it was increasingly rampant in the Surakarta, Cikampek, Semarang and Surabaya areas. The Dutch were increasingly pressed against the Japanese attack and Ooh finally the Dutch East Indies Government declared "unconditional surrender".

The stage of the sequence of events

The Indonesian people at first warmly welcomed the arrival of the Japanese military, it can be seen from the cooperative attitude of our national figures, Ir. Soekarno and Moh Hatta. The Japanese government began to actively embrace the people with the formation of community organizations, which in fact "there is a shrimp behind a rock" was actually behind it for the interests of Japan in World War II.

These organizations include: Tiga A Movement, People's Power Center (PUTERA), Jawa Hokokai, Seinendan, Keibodan, Fujinkai, Heiho, MIAI and the establishment of BPUPKI. BPUPKI (Indonesian Independence Preparatory Business Research Agency) was formed in 1943 under the government of Prime Minister Tojo, tasked with studying and investigating matters that were important and necessary for the formation of the Indonesian government.

In its subsequent development, BPUPKI was disbanded and replaced by our warrior figures, from BPUPKI to PPPKI or known as Docoritsu Junbi Inkai, with this name change the impression was that the PPPKI organization was not formed by Japan but the result of the agreement and struggle of Indonesian independence leaders.

An important event, namely the meeting of Soekarno, M. Hata and Rajiman Wedyodiningrat with General Terauchi in Dalat, said that the Japanese government had decided to grant independence to Indonesia, whose territory included the former Dutch East Indies.

The Japanese troops began to weaken, the defeats and defeats were obtained and America was getting stronger, especially after withdrawing its troops in Europe. The Japanese attack was stopped by American troops, among others, in May 1942 at the Battle of the Coral Sea and June 1942 at the Battle of Midway.

Japan was getting clattered because America went berserk so that on August 6, 1945 the US dropped its first Atomic Bomb on Hiroshima. America was not satisfied as well and three days later on August 9 the second Atomic Bomb landed again in the city of Nagasaki, two Japanese government city centers were destroyed to the ground.

Finally, Japan surrendered unconditionally to the Allies on August 14, 1945. The surrender was carried out on the Missouri ship on September 2, 1945 by Emperor Hirohito (Japan) and General Douglas Mc Arthur (Allies).
