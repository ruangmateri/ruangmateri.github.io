Pembahasan:

Offering help/service is an expression that is used to offer help or service. Jadi, offering help or service merupakan suatu ekpresi dalam bahasa inggris yang digunakan untuk menawarkan sebuah bantuan ataupun pelayanan. Ada beberapa bentuk kalimat ungkapan dari offering help or service, yaitu secara formal dan informal. Berikut ini pola kalimat dan contohnya:

Formal

May I + (action)?

e.g : May I help you?

Would you like me to + (action)?

e.g : Would you like me to make a tea?

Informal

Can I + (action)?

e.g : Can I open the window?

Do you want me + (action)?

e.g : Do you want me to reserve the table for you?

Adapun respon dari ungkapan diatas, yaitu menerima tawaran (accepting) dan menolak tawaran (declining). Berikut contoh ungkapan dari accepting and declining an offer:

Accepting:

Yes, please.Sound nice.Thank you. That would be really helpful.Yes, that would be great!Thank you. I would appreciate it.

Declining:

No, thank you.It's fine, thank youThanks but don't worry, I can do it by my self.I appreciate it but don't worry, I'm perfectly fine.
