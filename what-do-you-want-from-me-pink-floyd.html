15. She is ... beautiful pink drees from her mother


Jawaban:

Wearing

Penjelasan:

The correct verb to use in the blank is "wearing". So the sentence should be "She is wearing a beautiful pink dress from her mother."

The other verbs that could be used in the blank are "has" and "owns". However, these verbs would not be as accurate as "wearing" because they do not convey the sense that the dress is currently being worn.

For example, the sentence "She has a beautiful pink dress from her mother" could mean that the dress is currently in her closet or that she wore it recently. The sentence "She owns a beautiful pink dress from her mother" could mean that she has the dress in her possession, but it does not necessarily mean that she is wearing it right now.

The sentence "She is wearing a beautiful pink dress from her mother" is the most accurate because it clearly conveys that the dress is currently being worn.
