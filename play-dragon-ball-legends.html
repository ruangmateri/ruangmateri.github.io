15. I PLAY FOOT BALL WITH NY FRIENDS (+).....(-)......(?).....​


[Present Simple]

(+) I play football with my friends.

(-) I don't play football with my friends.

(?) Do I play football with my friends?

PEMBAHASAN

Simple Present Tense adalah bentuk kalimat yang berguna untuk menunjukkan hal yang terjadi sekarang, hal yang dilakukan berulang kali, serta suatu kebenaran umum.

↬Formula

Positive:

Subject + to be + O / CSubject + Verb 1 + O / C

Negative:

Subject + to be + not + O / CSubject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + O / C

Interrogative:

To be + subject + O / C + ?Do/does + subject + Verb 1 + O / C + ?

↬To Beis → untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)am → untuk subjek Iare → untuk subjek You/We/They dan subjek jamak

↬Auxiliary

do → untuk subjek I dan subjek jamak (You/We/They)does → untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)

↬Based on Question

Change into negative and interrogative!

I play football with my friends.

mengubah ke negatif (-) : letakkan aux/to be + not setelah subjekmengubah ke introgatif (?) : letakkan aux/to be sebelum subjek dan beri tanda tanya pada akhir kalimat

(+) I play football with my friends.

Aku bermain sepak bola bersama teman-temanku.

(-) I don't play football with my friends.

Aku tidak bermain sepak bola bersama teman-temanku

(?) Do I play football with my friends?

Apakah aku bermain sepak bola bersama teman-temanku?

29. (-)they are play ball in class (+) (?)


Jawaban:(+) They are playing ball in class. (-) They are not playing ball in class.(?) Are they playing ball in class?

PEMBAHASAN:

Sedikit koreksi di bagian kata kerja "play". Karena sebelum kata kerjanya terdapat to be "are" maka kata kerjanya harus berakhiran -ing.

Present Continuous

Tenses yang menjelaskan kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa sekarang.

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (am/are/is) + V-ing

She is studyingThey are eatingJohn is reading

(-) S + To be (am/are/is) + not + V-ing

She is not studyingThey are eatingJohn is reading

(?) To be (am/are/is) + S + V-ing

Is she studying? Are they eating? Is John reading?

Catatan :

Subject I => amSubject You/they/we/plural => areSubject she/he/it => is

Pada soal di atas kita ketahui bahwa :

➡ (+) They are playing ball in class. (Mereka sedang bermain bola di dalam kelas)

They => SubjectAre => To bePlaying => V-ingBall => ObjectIn class => Adverb of place

Maka bentuk negatif dan interrogatif nya :

(-) They are not playing ball in class. (Mereka tidak sedang bermain bola di dalam kelas)

(?) Are they playing ball in class? (Apakah mereka sedang bermain bola di dalam kelas?
