19. mary,........ to win the prize,tripped when she ran upstairs


Answer:


The sentence above is likely using relative pronoun in the gap as it is in the sub clause and since it is between commas or as an additional information, it is a non defining relative pronoun.

The relative pronouns are the word like who, which, where, whose that explains something (for defining) or adds extra information (for non-defining) in a complex sentence in a sub clause.

Here's the example of defining one:

- He bought a car which has been used for racing before.

(that is his one of his newly-bought cars and previously used for racing)

- He bought a car, which has been used for racing before.

( that is his one and only newly-bought car and previously used for racing)


Here's the answer:

- Mary, who was about to win the prize, tripped when she ran upstairs.

(in here we can see that 'who' is followed by 'was about' to be suitable with the context in the sentence and has the non-defining relative pronoun.


Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: relative pronoun

36. Mary, … to win the prize, tripped when she ran upstairs.


Mary, thrilling to win the prize, tripped when she ran upstairs. Kata thrilling tepat mengisi kalimat karena menyatakan kondisi aktif (dalam hal ini berdebar-debar) sehingga kata yang cocok mengisinya adalah V+ing.

Pembahasan

Kalimat ‘Mary, thrilling to win the prize, tripped when she ran upstairs’ jika diterjemahkan dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya adalah Mary, berdebar-debar (bisa juga bersemangat) untuk memenangkan hadiah, tersandung ketika berlari ke atas. Maksud kalimat ini adalah, Mary memenangkan perlombaan dan ketika ia berjalan naik ke atas panggung, Mary berdebar-debar (gugup atau terlalu bersemangat) yang membuat ia tak hati-hati sehingga tersandung.
