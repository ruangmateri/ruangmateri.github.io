28. angga...got some birthday gifts yesterday a. getb. getsc. got d. getting


Jawabannya adalah C. Got

✔️ angga got some birthday gifts yesterday

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

Past Tense berfungsi utk menyatakan suatu Kegiatan atau Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa lampau.

Keterangan waktu Past TenseYesterdaylast yeartwo days agoA few minutes ago

Rumus Past Tense(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O/C(?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O/C?

KESIMPULAN

Angga ... some birthday gifts yesterday.

a. getb. getsC. Got ✔️d. getting

CATATAN

Menggunakan Verb-2 “got” karena ada keterangan lampau “yesterday”
