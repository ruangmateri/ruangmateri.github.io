Jawaban:

1. different artinya adalah berbeda
Contoh kalimat:
Your house is different to mines.

2. natural artinya adalah alami
Contoh kalimat:
Your look is really natural.

3. cume artinya adalah manis
Contoh kalimat:
That sugar is really cume.

4. live artinya adalah tinggal (tinggal di rumah)
Contoh kalimat:

I live in Bekasi.

5. warm artinya adalah hangat
Contoh kalimat:
That tea is warm.

6. tiny artinya adalah sangat kecil  
Contoh kalimat:
The ant is tiny.

7. hole artinya adalah lubang
Contoh kalimat:
I always see a small hole in my bag.

8. inside artinya adalah di dalam (suatu gedung / rumah / bangunan)
Contoh kalimat:
My sister is always inside her room doing homework.

9. squeeze artinya adalah memencet (suatu benda) / meremas

Contoh kalimat:
I'm going to squeeze an orange to make juice.

10. mop up artinya adalah membersihkan cairan tumpah
Contoh kalimat:
My mom is mopping up all the spilled water.

29. what does bundle of Joy mean​


Jawaban:

The phrase "bundle of joy" is an idiomatic expression that is commonly used to refer to a newborn baby or a young child, particularly when they bring happiness, delight, and a sense of joy to their parents or family. It is often used in a positive and endearing manner to emphasize the happiness and excitement that a new baby brings into someone's life. The term "bundle" metaphorically suggests the idea of a small, precious package or gift that brings immense joy and happiness.
