Arti dari kalimat di atas adalah "Untuk pertunangannya, dia menginginkan cincin ..... di dunia" Jawaban yang dibutuhkan adalah berbentuk adjective.

Kata "The" yang terdapat pada sebelum bagian "in the world" menunjukkan bahwa kata yang dibutuhkan adalah "yang paling" dan merupakan bentuk superlative adjective.

Contoh:

The most beautiful : yang paling cantik/indah

The most handsome : yang paling tampan

Maka, jawaban dari kalimat rumpang di atas adalah the most expensive.

17. heal the world song expresses the writer's​


Understanding the Context from the Song

Heal the World song expresses the writer's concern about the world's condition. This song delivers a message that ​everyone can change the world no matter what you are and what you do. People can all do something to make the world better. For example, helping the poor people, keeping the environment, making the world more peaceful and happy and so on.

"There's a place in your heart" is an example of metaphor in the lyrics as heart is compared to a place. This helps connects the message of the song by saying that everyone has a heart for helping other people. It says that we should treat each other equally and stop the discrimination. This song shows how powerful one gesture of kindness really is and how someone can change the world and make it an enjoyable and loving place for everyone across the world.

Terjemahannya adalah sebagai berikut:

Lagu Heal the World mengekspresikan kekhawatiran penulis tentang kondisi dunia. Lagu ini menyampaikan pesan penulis bahwa setiap orang dapat mengubah dunia tidak peduli siapa pun kamu dan apa pun  pekerjaanmu. Semua orang dapat melakukan sesuatu untuk membuat dunia lebih baik. Misalnya, membantu orang miskin, menjaga lingkungan, membuat dunia lebih damai dan bahagia dan sebagainya.

"Ada tempat di hatimu" adalah contoh metafora dalam lirik karena hati dibandingkan dengan tempat. Ini membantu menghubungkan pesan lagu dengan mengatakan bahwa setiap orang memiliki hati untuk membantu orang lain. Dikatakan bahwa kita harus memperlakukan satu sama lain dengan setara dan menghentikan diskriminasi. Lagu ini menunjukkan betapa kuatnya satu gerakan kebaikan dan bagaimana seseorang dapat mengubah dunia dan menjadikannya tempat yang menyenangkan dan penuh kasih bagi semua orang di seluruh dunia.

32. what is the theme of the song heal the world


The song 'Heal the World' by Michael Jackson is one of the most famous songs about world peace. This song is frequently used for world peace campaign and event or advertisement of such kind. The theme is obviously about promoting peace around the world.

Penjelasan:

To find out the theme of the song is not that hard to do, especially in this digital era. There are several pointers to see, they are

the part of the lyrics in itthe tone of the song combined with the whole meaning in lyricsthe music video, which is the easiest one.

Here are the parts of the lyrics of the song, which was released in 1991, that indicates the theme of the song:

heal the world/make a better place/ for you and for me/ and the entire human race.There are people dying / if you care enough for the living.make a better place for you and for me.

If you ever see its music video, it does clearly show the clips of people around the world who suffer from famine, poverty and war. In the early 1990s, the world was in a critical time. There were a number of events which concerned the whole world such as the Tianamen Tragedy in China, Gulf War 2 in Middle East, The fall of communism which led to the fall of Berlin wall etc. Those events had cost the life of a lot of people.

To sum up, the song is about how to make the world better, promote world peace and help out people who are in agony.
