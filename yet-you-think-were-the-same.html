5. which one is the present perfect tense sentence?Awill have you read the book yet?Bhave you read the book yet?Cwill you read the book yet?Dhad been you reading the book yet?​


Jawabannya dalah B.

Formula untuk present perfect tense adalah: Have/Has + V3 + Object + Complement.

Penjelasan lebih lanjut:

Opsi A adalah Present future perfect, karena ada kata will

Opsi B adalah Present Perfect, dimana ada kata Have dan V3(Yaitu read)

Opsi C adalah Present Future

Opsi D adalah Past Perfect Continous

Identify the expressions below!

Identifikasi ungkapan di bawah ini!

Pembahasan

1. Asking and Giving Opinion

➡ Asking opinion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan pendapat. Berikut ungkapannya:

what do you think about...?what do you feel/think about....?do you think that.....?do you feel that...?what's your opinion about that...?how do you feel about...?don't you think that...

➡ Giving Opinion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan pendapat.

in my opinion...i think that...i believe that....if you ask me,i think that...i'm sure that...personally,i believe...I really think that...

2. Expression of Agreement

➡ Asking Agreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan persetujuan.

do you agree that....?how about you...

➡ Agreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan memberikan persetujuan.

I agree with you...i think you're right...i feel the same way...that' what i think...absolutely,i agree...that's a good point'...

➡ Disagreement adalah ungkapan yang digunakan memberikan ketidaksetujuan.

sorry, I don't think that's truee...i don't agree with you...i don't feel the same way...yes,but...i see it differently.

The mysterious man, on the other hand, might experience relief and a sense of personal growth from changing his life for the better.

2) The moral value of the story is that acts of kindness and compassion can have a profound impact on people's lives. The mysterious man's selfless act of saving the narrator's life not only changed the narrator's perspective but also inspired him to adopt a more positive and faith-filled outlook. The story emphasizes the transformative power of human kindness and encourages readers to appreciate and embrace acts of compassion in their own lives.
