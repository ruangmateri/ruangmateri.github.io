PEMBAHASAN:

An opinion is the way you feel or think about something. Our opinion about something or someone is based on our perspective. Whenever we give or express our opinion it is important to give reasoning or an example to support our opinion.

Asking Opinion

What do you think  …? Do you agree with me? Don’t you think(that) …? What  are you thought on that? Would you agree with me that …? Do you see what I’m getting at? Do you know /see what I mean? Etc..  

Giving Opinion

I think … To my mind … I (really) feel (that) … As for me, I reckon … I (honestly) think that … From my point of view  … In my opinion …/In my view … Personally speaking, I believe …

Thanks, Julia.Julia : Anytime, Drue!From the dialogue above we know that Julia is...A. giving orderB. giving an opinionC. asking for an opinionD. asking for attention​


From the dialogue above we know that Julia is giving an opinion (B). Jadi dalam dialog di atas kita mengetahui bahwa Julia memberikan opini (giving an opinion).

Pembahasan

Dalam dialog singkat di atas bila kita baca maka temanya adalah asking and giving opinion (meminta dan memberikan pendapat). Bila kita terjemahkan dialog di atas maka akan menjadi:

Drue: Bolehkah saya tahu pendapatmu tentang cerita pendek saya?

Julia: Ceritanya bagus, tapi saya sarankan ganti judulnya dengan

judul yang menarik.

Drue: Pendapat yang bagus. Terima kasih, Julia.

Julia: Kapan saja, Drue!

Kita perhatikan dalam dialog tersebut Drue meminta pendapat Julia dengan kalimat:

May I know what do you think of my short story?

Atau bisa juga dengan kalimat yang lebih singkat:

What do you think of my short story?

Lalu Julia memberikan pendapatnya (opinion) dengan kalimat:

I suggest changing the title with an interesting title.

Pilihan jawaban lain tidak tepat karena:

A. Giving order artinya memberikan perintah.

C. Asking for an opinion artinya meminta pendapat.

D. Asking for attention​ artinya meminta perhatian.

Expressing opinion adalah cara kita merasakan atau memikirkan sesuatu. Pendapat kita tentang sesuatu atau seseorang didasarkan pada perspektif kita.

Contoh:

Asking Opinion

What do you think  …? Do you agree with me? Don’t you think(that) …? What  are you thought on that? Would you agree with me that …? Do you see what I’m getting at? Do you know /see what I mean? Etc..  

Giving Opinion

I think … To my mind … I (really) feel (that) … As for me, I reckon … I (honestly) think that … From my point of view  … In my opinion …/In my view … Personally speaking, I believe …
