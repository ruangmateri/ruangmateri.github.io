33. 9. Q:Do you enjoy playing in the park? A: ... a.No,I doesn't enjoy playing the park b.No,I no enjoy playing the park c.No,I don't enjoy playing the park d.No,I don't enjoys playing the park10. Q:Does he love his mother? A: ... a.He love his mother b.He loves his mother c.He to love his mother d.He is loving his mother TOLONGG!! ​


Jawaban:

9.c

10.b

Penjelasan:

ini materi tentang simple present

Kalimat "do you enjoy playing in the park?" berarti "apakah kamu menikmati bermain di taman? Kalimat negatif dalam bentuk Simple Present dimulai dengan subjek kemudian do/does, diikuti not dan kata kerja dasar (V1). Maka, tanggapan yang tepat adalah "no, I don't enjoy playing in the park".

Jadi, jawabannya adalah C. no, I don't enjoy. playing in the park.

sedangkan untuk no 10. Kalimat positif dalam bentuk Simple Present dimulai dengan subjek dan diikuti kata kerja (s/es). Pada soal ini, subjeknya adalah orang ketiga tunggal "he", sehingga kata kerja love mendapat akhiran -s. Tanggapan yang sesuai adalah he loves his mother.

Jadi, jawabannya adalah B. he loves his mother.
