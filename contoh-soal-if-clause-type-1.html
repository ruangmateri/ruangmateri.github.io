36. Contoh soal conditional clause dong yg type 1, 2 ,3 . 15 soal yaa sama jawabannya :D


Type 1
1) If Caroline and Sue ___(prepare) the salad, Phil ___(decorate) the house.
prepare/will decorate
2) If Sue ___(cut) the onions for the salad, Caroline ___(peel) the mushrooms.
cuts//will peel
3) Jane ___(hoover) the sitting room if Aaron and Tim ___(move) the furniture.
will hoover//move
4) If Bob ___(tidy up) the kitchen, Anita ___(clean) the toilet.
tidies//will clean
5) Elaine ___(buy) the drinks if somebody ___(help) her carry the bottles.
will buy//helps

Type 2
6) We ___(help) you if we ___(know) how.
would help//knew
7) My brother ___(buy) a sports car if he ___(have) the money.
would buy//had
8) If I ___(feel) better, I ___(go) to the cinema with you.
felt//would go
9) If you ___(go) by bike more often, you ___(be / not) be so flabby.
went//would not
10) She (not / talk) to you if she ___(be) mad at you.
would not//were

Type 3
11) If the goalie ___(jump) up, he ___(catch) the ball.
had jumped//would have caught
12) If the forwards ___(run) faster, they ___(score) more goals.
had run//would have scored
13) If it ___(have) a home game, our team ___(win) the match.
had been//would have won
14) If you ___(ask) me, I ___(help) you.
had asked//would have helped
15) If you ___(speak) English, she ___(understand).
had spoken//would have understood)
