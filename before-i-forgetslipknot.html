40. 4. My father had eaten before I worked.A. Hadn't my father eaten before !worked?B. Hadn't my father ate before I worked?C. Had my father not eat before I worked?D. Has my father not eat before I worked?Pakai rumus Past perfect​


Jawaban:

A. Hadn't my father eaten before worked?

Pembahasan

Pas Perfect adalah Tenses yg menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu dan selesai di Kegiatan di masa lalu juga.

Berdasarkan Rumus:

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

My father had eaten before I worked.

(-) S + had not + V3 + Complement

My father had not eaten before I worked.

(?) had + S + V3 + Complement

had My father eaten before I worked.?

(-?) had not/hadn’t + S + V3 + Complement?

A. Hadn't my father eaten before worked? ✅Opsi ini tepat karena sesuai Rumus

Sedangkan pilihan ganda lainnya:

B. Hadn't my father ate before I worked?

Opsi ini salah karena menggunakan Verb 2 (ate)

C. Had my father not eat before I worked?

Opsi C salah karena menggunakan Verb 1 (eat)

D. Has my father not eat before I worked?

Opsi ini salah karena menggunakan has +Verb 1 (eat)
