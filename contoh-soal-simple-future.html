Simple Future :

S + will + V1

- Examples :

1. I will cut my hair tomorrow

2. She will go to the market next week

3. He will buy a bicycle tomorrow

4. They will watch a movie tomorrow

5. We will take a test next Tuesday


Future Continous :

S + will + be + Ving

- Examples :

1. I will be cutting my hair in the afternoon

2. She will be going to the market this time next week

3. He will be buying a bicycle this time tomorrow

4. They will be watching a movie tonight

5. We will be taking a test this time next Tuesday


Future Perfect :

S + will + have + V3

-Examples :

1. I will have cut my hair

2. She will have gone to the market

3. He will have bought a bicycle

4. They will have watched a movie

5. We will have taken a test

19. Beda simple future tense dan simple future continuous tense apa ya? kalau bisa beritau rumus dan contohnya juga. terimakasih​


Jawaban:

Simple future tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang akan terjadi pd waktu yang akan datang.

Rumus :

(+) S + shall/will + v1

(-) S + shall/will+ not + v1

(?) Shall/will + v1 ?

Contoh :

(+) I will come to your house tonight.

(-) I will not come to your house tonight.

(?) Will I come to your house tonight?

Future continuous tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang akan terjadi pada suatu waktu di masa yg akan datang.

Rumus :

(+) S + shall/will + be + v1 + ing.

(-) S + shall/will + not + be + v1 + ing.

(?) Shall/will + be + v1 + ing?

Contoh :

(+) We shall be sleeping if you come to my house at midnight.

(-) we shall not be sleeping if you come to my house at midnight.

(?) Shall we be sleeping if you come to my house at midnight?

20. Contoh kalimat simple future, future continues, future perfect verbal dan nominal


1. Simple Future
a) Verbal: I will watch TV tomorrow.
b) Nominal: I will be at home tomorrow.

2. Future Continuous
a) Verbal: I will be watching TV tomorrow at 07.00 a.m.
b) Nominal: I will be at home tomorrow at 07.00 a.m.

3. Future Perfect
a) Verbal: I will have watched TV by tomorrow.
b) Nominal: I will have been at home by tomorrow.

Contoh Kalimat Dalam Bentuk Simple Future Tense :

1. (+) We shall go together. (Kami akan pergi bersama-sama)
    (-)  We shan't go together. (Kami tidak akan pergi bersama-sama)
    (?)  Shall we go together? (Apakah kami akan pergi bersama-sama?)
          Jawaban : Yes, we shall (Ya) atau No, we shan't (Tidak) 

2. (+) They will come to the party tonight. (Mereka akan datang ke pesta nanti malam.)
    (-) They won't come to the party tonight. (Mereka tidak akan datang ke pesta nanti malam.)
    (?) Will they come to the party tonight? (Apakah mereka akan datang ke pesta nanti malam?)
          Jawaban : Yes, they will (Ya) atau No, they won't(Tidak) 

3. (+) He will buy a new bag tomorrow. (Dia akan membeli sebuah tas besok.)
    (-) He won't buy a new bag tomorrow. (Dia tidak akan membeli sebuah tas besok.)
    (?) Will he buy a new bag tomorrow? (Apakah dia akan membeli sebuah tas besok?)
         Jawaban : Yes, he will (Ya) atau No, he won't (Tidak).

rumus dari simple future tense ada 3 :

1. Subject + will +verb 1/be (rumus ini untuk plan yang spontan, jd tidak terencana)

2. Subject + is/am/are + going + to + verb 1 (untuk plan yang terencana)

3. Subject + is/am/are + verb ing ( untuk plan yang terencana)
