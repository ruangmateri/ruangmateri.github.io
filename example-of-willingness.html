2. What is willingness?? Can you give me 5 example of willingness?!


Willingness is (in Indonesia: Kerelaan) the quality or state of being prepared to do something; readiness.  

synonyms:

readiness, inclination, will, wish, desire, alacrity  

"we appreciate your willingness to help"


Examples:

- "the ability and willingness of workers to migrate"

- "It also expressed willingness that the companies should have rights of way in the streets"

- "Many unfounded rumours of a willingness on the part of the Confederate States to make peace were circulated to weaken the Union war spirit"

- "On the other hand, there were signs of a greater willingness among the Socialists to co-operate with their old enemies the Liberals"

- "He soon showed a willingness to rule as a non-partisan, and favoured the re-enfranchisement of white citizens"

7. mencari pengertian & contoh Expression OF ability and willingness.


Jawaban:

1. Ability

Capability artinya kemampuan/kesanggupan untuk melakukan/mengerjakan sesuatu. Untuk mengekspresikan kemampuan atau kapabilitas dalam Bahasa Inggris, kita dapat menggunakan modal “can, could, atau be able” yang artinya “dapat/bisa”

Contoh: I can climb a tree

2. Willingness

Willingness berarti kesediaan, kemauan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Untuk menyatakan itu kita dapat menggunakan modal –will/would. Yang berarti akan/mau/sedia.

Will dan would memiliki kesamaan. Perbedaannya adalah Will digunakan untuk bentuk present tense (Sekarang) dan would untuk bentuk lampau.

Contoh: a. I will come to your house. b. He would go there

33. Apa rumus expression of willingness


In the present: will, won’t

Kita dapat menggunakan will untuk mengatakan bahwa willing to do something

Tom's a really kind person; he will always help you if you need something.

We can use will not / won't to express an unwillingness to do something:

Kita bisa menggunakan tidak / tidak akan mengungkapkan keengganan untuk melakukan sesuatu:

Ketika berbicara tentang masa lalu, kita dapat menggunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa seseorang pada umumnya bersedia melakukan sesuatu:

David doesn't want to talk to me – he won't answer the phone.

My car won't start – I'll have to take the bus.

In the past: would, wouldn’t

David tidak mau berbicara dengan saya - dia tidak akan menjawab telepon.

Mobil saya tidak mau menyala - saya harus naik bus.

Di masa lalu: akan, tidak akan

39. Berikan contoh dialog the expression of willingness and unwillingness!


Berikut adalah contoh dialog yang berisikan expression of willingness and unwillingness.

Mr. Raimy: Hi Juan! How are you today?

Juan: Hello Mr. Raimy, I'm fine but a little tired today.

Mr. Raimy: Would you mind telling me what did you do today?

Juan: Yes I will, so this morning I did my school project as usual but then my parents asked me to help clean the house because there will be guests coming in the evening. We finished clean the house by noon and then had lunch. After that I continued doing my school project and now here I am.

Mr. Raimy: Wow you really had a busy day. Will you tell me about your school project that you worked on?

Juan: No, I won't. I am so sorry Mr. Raimy but I am so exhausted. Can I just lay down on your couch for 30 minutes before our practice?

Mr. Raimy: Of course, you can take a nap too if you will.

Juan: I will! Thank you so much Mr. Raimy.

Pembahasan

Expression of willingness and unwillingness memiliki makna secara harfiah yaitu ekspresi kesediaan dan keengganan. Maksud dari willingness adalah rasa kemauan/kerelaan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Sebaliknya unwillingness adalah rasa ketidakmauan seseorang untuk melakukan sesuatu. Untuk menyatakan ekspresi-ekspresi tersebut dalam bahasa Inggris dapat menggunakan modal auxiliary verb yaitu will dalam bentuk simple present atau menggunakan would dalam bentuk simple past. Berikut adalah aturan expression of willingness and unwillingness:

Untuk menyatakan willingnessS + will/would (like to) + V1 + O+ adv: I will take you to the doctor.S + to be + willing to + V1 + O + adv: She is willing to do the dishes later.Untuk menyatakan unwillingnessS + will/would + not + V1 + O + adv: Danny would not tell me about his girlfriend.Untuk menanyakan willingnessWill + S + V1 + O + adv + ?: Will you come tonight?Would + S + (like to) + V1 + O + adv + ?: Would Mary like to help in the kitchen?
