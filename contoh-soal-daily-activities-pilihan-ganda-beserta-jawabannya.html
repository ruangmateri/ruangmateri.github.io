6. CONTOH DAILY ACTIVITIES DARI BANGUN TIDUR SAMPAI MAU TIDUR


Contoh daily activities dari bangun tidur sampai mau tidur yaitu

      I usually wake up at five in the morning. I make my bed and then take a bath. My mother usually asks me to go the market buying vegetables. After going to the market, I help my mother cooking and prepare our breakfast. We have breakfast at six in the morning.

During the pandemic, my activities are just at home. After having breakfast, I water the plant and then play game on my handphone. At a half past seven, I study by online. I finish at a quarter to twelve.

After studying, I help my mother to prepare our lunch. We have lunch at one in the afternoon. After that, I play with my handphone and sometimes, I take a nap for a while.

At three in the afternoon, I watch television with my sister. At five in the afternoon, I take a bath and play with my handphone again. At a half past seven in the evening, we have dinner together. And then, I play again. I usually go to the bed at nine in the night.

Pembahasan

Arti dari teks di atas adalah

Saya biasanya bangun pada jam 5 pagi hari. Saya merapikan tempat tidur dan kemudian mandi. Ibu saya biasanya meminta saya ke pasar membeli sayuran. Setelah pergi ke pasar, saya membantu ibu saya memasak dan menyiapkan sarapan kami. Kami sarapan pada jam 6 pagi hari.

Selama pandemi, aktivitas saya hanya di rumah. Setelah sarapan, saya menyirami tanaan dan kemudian bermain gim di handphone. Pada jam 08.30, saya belajar daring. Saya selesai pada jam 11.45.

Setelah belajar, saya membantu ibu saya menyiapkan makan siang. Kami makan siang jam 1 siang. Setelah itu, saya bermain dengan HP saya dan kadang-kadang, saya tidur siang untuk beberapa saat.

Pada jam 3 siang hari, saya menonton TV bersama saudara perempuan saya. Pada jam 5, saya mandi dan bermain HP lagi. Pada pukul 06.30, kami makan malam bersama. Dan kemudian, saya bermain HP lagi. Saya biasanya tidur pada jam 9 malam.

9. Tolong buatkan text dialog daily activity dengan 3 soal pilihan ganda. Terimakasih


Daily activity adalah kegiatan atau aktivitas yang dilakukan setiap hari. Daily activity bisa juga diartikan sebagai kebiasaaan sehari-hari.

Dalam membuat sebuah daily activity, kita harus menggunakan simple present tense. Itu karena simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kebiasaan.

Simple present tense mempunyai formula:

S + V1 atau S + V1s/es


V1 untuk I, You, They, dan We

Vis/es untuk He, she dan it.


Pembahasan

Membuat percakapan tentang daily activity beserta 3 soal pilihan ganda:

Teacher: Hello everyone. Today, we are going to discuss about daily activity. First of all, I will ask you what do you do everyday.

Teacher: Rina, what do you do at 6 o'clock every morning?

Rina: I take a bath at 6 o'clock every morning mam.

Teacher: Good. Next, Anton, what do you do every afternoon?

Anton: I always take a nap every afternoon mam. School activity consumes my energy. I need to take a rest after that.

Teacher: Okay Anton, nice answer. Next, Marisa, What do you do before sleeping?

Marisa: I always make my bed, wash my hands and feet and have a pray before sleeping mam. I ask God to protect me during my sleeping.

Teacher: You do it well Marisa. Okay, please open your book on the page 25. We will start the lesson.

3. What does Marisa do before sleeping, except?

Then, I prepare some books based on schedule. I do not want to leave any book at home. I wash my face, brush my teeth, then have a shower. Finally, I eat breakfast after wearing my school uniform.
