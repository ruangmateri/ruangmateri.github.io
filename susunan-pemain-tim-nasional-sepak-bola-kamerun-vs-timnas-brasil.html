Kiper AS Roma, Alisson Becker (AFP/Michael B. Thomas)

Penyerang PSG menggandeng pemain Bordeaux, Malcom. (AFP Photo/Franck Fife)

Pemain Liverpool, Philippe Coutinho, (AP/Rui Vieira)
